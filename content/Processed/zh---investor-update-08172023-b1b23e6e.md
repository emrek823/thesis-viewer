## Slide 3: The confluence of market opportunity, demographics and our current assets makes now an ideal time for Zocalo Health to grow through health plan partnerships
### Demographics
**The Latino Population is Large and Growing Rapidly**
* Latinos currently represent 18% of the U.S. population
* Latinos are projected to represent 30% of the population by 2050
* 1/3 of Latinos have had zero access to healthcare

### Market Opportunities
**CMS Changes Incent Payers to Focus On their Latino Patients**
* Changes to STARs will specifically demand for culturally aligned services
* New CHW reimbursement opportunities
* Medicaid redeterminations will grow Medicare demand
* Disparities add to payer costs

### Assets
**We are Well-Positioned to Provide High Quality Care to Latinos**
* We developed a culturally aligned care model that boosts engagement and outcomes
* The ZH community engagement model boosts Latino participation with plans and in their own health care

## Slide 4: We built key assets and established key learnings with the D2C model, but we recognize superior opportunities for growth
### Direct to Consumer Care
**2021 - 2023**
* Limited TAM: the D2C Model excludes many Latinos due to its high cost for patients
* Long Ramp: over 12 months we engaged 483 members of the Latino communities across three states
* Episodic: 70% of visits were one-time
* High CAC: $244

### Learnings & Foundational Assets
**2023**
* **Learnings**
    * The smallest operating unit for Latinos is the family
    * Cultural competence and community engagement are the keys to growth and long-term cost saving
* **Foundational Assets**
    * A technical platform for
    * A forward-looking product vision
    * A model and playbook for providing culturally proficient care
    * A robust payer partnership pipeline

we are here

### Deliver Care to Insured Latinos
**2024 - 2025**
* Build in-network FFS positions in commercial Medicare & Medicaid
* Validate, TAM, CAC, and revenue assumptions
* Finalize & deploy market entry playbook for contractual agreements beyond FFS
* Build network capabilities in select MSAs (aligned with target MSA for 2024)
* Establish extensive health plan pipeline

### Establish VBC Contracts
**≥2026**
* Build or buy the care management and technical capabilities necessary to accept risk based contracts
* Accept patient delegation with a focus on Medicare plans
* Execute risk bearing contract, up to and including capitation
* Increased focus on primary Latino markets (CA, TX, AZ, FL) in addition to secondary markets
