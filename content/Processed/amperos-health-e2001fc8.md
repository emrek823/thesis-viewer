# Amperos Health Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Amperos Health - AI coworkers that get claims paid

## Slide 2: Problem - Healthcare providers struggle to get paid

*   Obstacles imposed by insurance companies include asking for documentation, creating unique rules, not providing updates on claim status, and more
*   Most of the administrative burden is related to **denied claims**, which clinics address today by having staff call insurance, write letters, fill out forms, etc.

## Slide 3: How Big is the Problem?

*   **$9bn+** Administrative spend by healthcare systems on appeals
*   **$90bn** Recoverable revenue denied by insurance to hospitals alone
*   **20%** Increase in denials in last 2 years
*   **12%** Of claims denied
*   **~45 min** To process complex appeals
*   **$118** Administrative costs per appeal

Source: User interviews, AMA, Change Healthcare

## Slide 5: What's the Solution?

### Vision
*   Team of AI coworkers, each tailored to a specific denial workflow
*   "Sits on top" of existing customer software
*   LLM tailored to reflect best strategy for a specific sub-specialty and payor

### Result
*   Billing staff transforms from "doers" to strategic decision makers
*   Time to process a denial goes from 20-45 min to <1 min
*   Slash cost to collect, decrease AR days, increase revenue
*   AI reflects tactics used by best billers

## Slide 10: Product Development Roadmap

*   **Insurance Calling** -> **Existing LLM**
*   Refine Model Through Collected Data
*   **Insurance Forms** -> **Appeal Letters** -> **Payer Policy Engine** -> **Decision Making** -> **End-to-End Coworker Experience**
*   **Proprietary Model** (derived from refined model and collected data)
*   End result is each biller has a team of coworkers, each specific to a task, working with little intervention
*   Increased data capture allows for model fine tuning, then training own model, increasing moat

## Slide 12: Business Model

### AI Calling
*   Initially priced per minute, with minimum cost per call.

### New Coworkers
*   Each AI coworker product will be priced based on size and complexity of enterprise customer

*   Aim to price at ~20% of value of recovered revenue
