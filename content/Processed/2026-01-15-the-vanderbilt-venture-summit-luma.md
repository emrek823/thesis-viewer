About Event

​We are excited to invite you to the inaugural **Vanderbilt Venture Summit**, hosted by the Vanderbilt University Office of Investments in Nashville, TN!

​This exclusive gathering will convene an intentionally curated group of leading GPs from exceptional venture firms, both current partners within our portfolio and highly regarded peers in our broader network, alongside a select group of like-minded LPs. Each attendee shares our focus on emerging, disciplined, and differentiated managers.

​The Summit is designed to spark substantive dialogue, deepen authentic relationships, and create space for real collaboration across institutions. Programming will blend structured conversations with ample time for informal connection. Additional details on the agenda, speakers, and sessions will be shared as we get closer to the event.

​We look forward to hosting you here in Nashville!
