## Summary

Veeva Systems reported Q1 2024 results exceeding guidance, with total revenue at $526 million.  Key initiatives include the development of Vault CRM, new Compass data products, and AI applications like CRM Bot. They reiterated their guidance for fiscal year 2025, with revenue expected to be at least $2.8 billion and operating income of at least $1 billion.
