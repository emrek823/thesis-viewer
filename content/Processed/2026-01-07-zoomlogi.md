About ZoomLogi

Founded to solve one problem: critical healthcare shipments still run on spreadsheets, phone calls, and guesswork. At ZoomLogi, we build an AI-native operating system that watches every shipment in real time, predicts risks like weather, delays, or customs holds, and automates the calls, emails, and workflows needed to keep product safe. With automation at the core, our platform helps pharma, biotech, and logistics teams scale operations and deliver life-saving therapies on time, without scaling teams size.

## What we believe and build toward!

We believe in patient-first logistics. Our mission is to make every critical shipment visible, predictable, and on time, so potentially life-saving therapies reliably reach the people who need them.

### Our Mission

We’re on a mission to take the guesswork out of healthcare logistics. ZoomLogi gives ops teams real-time visibility, risk predictions, and AI-powered workflows so they spend less time chasing status updates and more time ensuring product integrity and patient outcomes.

Remove manual status chasing with real-time, exception-driven workflows

Help teams scale volume and quality without adding headcount
