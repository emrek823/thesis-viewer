## STATEMENT OF WORK

Customer shall receive twenty-five (25) user licenses to the Ryght, a secure, private, generativeAI platform specifically designed for life sciences.

Ryght shall support customer on-boarding, customer training, and general customer support in an effort for Customer users to maximize their experience with the platform.

Ryght shall ensure disease state specific collections sourced from Pubmed for breast, bladder, and prostate cancer are available within the platform. Additional disease states can be added by customer request.

Ryght shall continuously add industry relevant templates for the creation of documents related to the planning and execution of clinical trials.

Upon Customer request Ryght shall securely build data connectors to proprietary Customer data. Users will then be able to leverage the dataset for search & retrieval and content generation.

Ryght platform improvements will happen continuously and through an iterative process.
