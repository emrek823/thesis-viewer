## Slide 12: Why Now?

Why Now?

### Regulatory Tailwinds
*   Broader medicare dental coverage gets push under Biden's administration as "medically necessary" services
*   Many states including Massachusetts are now looking into requiring companies to maintain DLR of ~70-80%

### Growth in Market
*   Since 2015, ~15% increase in dental benefit penetration reaching 80% in 2020
*   50.1M Medicare beneficiaries have increased coverage through medicare supplement and medicare advantage options

### Shift to Value-Based Care
*   Ongoing shift from volume based reimbursement to value based reimbursement in government programs
*   By year 2030, CMS mandates shift from fee for service (FFS) to value-based care (VBC) contracting
*   32Health is uniquely positioned to embrace value-based contracting models through a flexible techstack that enables VBS contracting & payment models

32Health's intelligent automated administration platform will help insurance companies to be competitive in reducing the cost of administration and overcome the regulatory requirements
