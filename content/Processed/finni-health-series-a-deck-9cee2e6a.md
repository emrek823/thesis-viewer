## Slide 4: Problem

Providers want to leave these clinics, but it is financially and
operationally complex.

*   **Contracting Struggles**
    It isn't easy to get group contracts and the process will take upwards of 180 days.
*   **Expensive Software**
    It costs on average $800/mo for all the software you need to run an autism care clinic.
*   **Hiring Staff to Deliver Care**
    Unique to autism care, you need to hire staff that delivers the direct one-to-one therapy.
*   **Managing Working Capital**
    Providers would need to float up to $150,000 of working capital to keep their company alive.
