## Summary

Zimmer Biomet's Q4 results were impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic, but the company demonstrated progress on strategic and operational fronts. They announced a plan to spin-off Spine and Dental businesses into a new company (NewCo) to enhance focus and growth for both entities, and believe it will accelerate their path to 4-5% growth and 30% operating margin by the end of 2023.
