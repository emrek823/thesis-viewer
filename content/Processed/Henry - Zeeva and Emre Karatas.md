### Product & Problem Solution

- Ziva is an “Uber-like” marketplace for home health:
	- Connects clinicians and agencies in real-time
	- AI-powered documentation assistance
	- Matches patients to nearest available clinicians
	- EHR agnostic system with speech-to-text capabilities
- Core problems addressed:
	- Documentation time reduction (from 90 mins to under 15 mins)
	- Inefficient clinician-patient matching
	- Excessive drive times for clinicians
	- Staffing shortages
