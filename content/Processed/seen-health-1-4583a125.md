## Slide 3: Primary care is failing over 70% of the American public
Primary care is failing over
70% of the American public

**Healthcare avoidance**
69% of adults who have obesity or are overweight have experienced weight-related stigma from their doctor, leading to avoidance of primary care

**Inadequate treatment**
For those patients who enter primary care, the disease of obesity is not managed (1.3% of eligible patients receive a weight loss medication prescription, as PCP's do not feel adequately trained)

**Worsening health**
This care leads to lack of preventative screenings, more advanced and poorly controlled chronic disease, and low health-related quality of life

**Higher costs**
An expensive population is more likely to visit the ER, doctor shop, and spend more on direct healthcare costs

## Slide 8: SEEN will leverage obesity medicine to recruit patients and create value
SEEN will leverage obesity medicine
to recruit patients and create value

This model leverages the unique dynamic of obesity medicine: the supply-constrained specialty typically refers to primary care, not the other way around

*   **A Behavioral health, nutrition, and remote patient monitoring:** A comprehensive offering to help patients with their goals, all billable to insurance
*   **B Robust primary care:** With physicians trained, by us, in the disease of obesity to help scale the practice
*   **C Specialty pharmacy:** Owned by SEEN health, delivering convenience for patients and margin for SEEN
*   **D Curated referral network:** Specialists opt into SEEN training to qualify as a referral partner, ensuring our patients receive great care by staying within our network of providers
*   **E Bariatric surgery:** We take risk on patients through a bariatric bundle, and for those who ultimately pursue weight loss surgery, we provide their long-term care

## Slide 9: For patients, the PCP will be their quarterback
For patients, the PCP will be their
quarterback

*   **High EQ, obesity trained primary care doctor who acts as the quarterback for patient treatment**
*   **Modern, convenient physical offices** with size-inclusive equipment, thoughtful layouts (e.g., no public weighing), in-house labs, and specialty pharmacy
*   **Integrated digital experience,** offering remote patient monitoring, telemedicine, quick access to care team, prescription refills, coaching, and a community forum
*   **In-house obesity medicine physicians** for those patients wanting the latest in weight loss and metabolic disease treatment; once initial treatment is completed, patients will be managed through the primary care physician
*   **In-house registered medical dietitian and medical social worker** for those interested in working with the obesity medicine physician, group therapy, and to ensure all patients have access to comprehensive obesity treatment
*   **Carefully curated list of external specialists** (e.g., cardiologists) who are willing work closely with the practice and undergo basic training
*   **Community of local and national patients** who support each other
