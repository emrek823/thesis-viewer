### Company Overview

- $400K raised to date
- CTO is executive at Intuit
- Operating costs are $14K/month (engineering team of 5 in Thailand)
- Built as a filtration system for massive data companies
- Developed by former medical reps who understand user needs

### Product Value Proposition

- Provides intelligence for medical sales reps
- Focuses on closing doctors rather than just identifying them
- Integrates internet information, competitor research, and claims data
- Creates custom sales approaches in 30 seconds for any US physician

### Three Pillars of Medical Sales

- Understanding target physicians
	- Contact information (60% email capture rate)
	- Affiliations and notable colleagues
	- Publications related to specific products
- Mastering products
	- Correlates physician research with products
	- Integrates marketing materials and white papers
	- Generates custom talk tracks from articles
- Fitting products with practice
	- Creates personalized sales approaches
	- Identifies best product leads based on physician data
	- Optimizes 3-5 minute sales windows

### RepPrep vs AcuityMD Comparison

- RepPrep advantages:
	- 80% utilization rate (60% higher than Acuity)
	- 35% increase in closing rates across two trials
	- Teams functionality with broader access
- AcuityMD limitations:
	- High price point restricts full sales team deployment
	- Focuses on team leads rather than individual reps
	- Limited to “trickle down” information flow
	- No teams account capability

### Pricing Structure

- Current pricing:
	- $99/month or $1000/year (base model with Medicare data)
	- Planning separate versions with Purple Labs partnership
	- Enhanced version with commercial claims/updated Medicare will be ~50% higher
	- Remains one-fifth the cost of Acuity even with premium tier
