## STATEMENT OF WORK

*   Ryght shall develop co-pilots with/for Customer (see description below) leveraging its proprietary Ryght Data Pipeline.
*   Ryght Data Pipeline means, a proprietary application that ingests unstructured data through secure connection, optimizes generative artificial intelligence language model selection and orchestration, prompt strategy, data preprocessing, and is capable of multiple data output formats. Ryght Data Pipeline encrypts data in-transit (TLS1.3) and at rest (AES256).
*   For clarity, PII means Personally Identifiable Information as defined in NIST Special Publication 800-122.
*   For clarity, Receipt means a single transaction receipt that may or may not contain PII comprising a single common image file format or PDF.
*   Customer shall also receive 25 user licenses for access to the Ryght platform for day-to-day use.
*   Initial copilot will be PII Identification and Redaction co-pilot:
    *   Receipt data integration from Customer to Ryght Data Pipeline:
        *   Discover necessary data sources for interpolation
        *   Determine secure methods for connecting sources
        *   If necessary, develop connectors
        *   Structure data for access
    *   Ryght Data Pipeline receipt processing
        *   Ingest receipts from Customer
        *   Identify if PII is present
        *   Redact PII when appropriate
            *   Customer and Ryght shall collaborate to determine method of PII obfuscation
                *   Extraction of receipt data and redacting PII vs displaying original receipt image with PII blurred or covered
                    *   Extraction of receipt data and redacting PII is Ryght's preferred method for accuracy and stability, however both parties recognize third party contract research organization customer will need to approve
            *   Ryght shall only store receipts during Ryght Data Pipeline processing timeframe
    *   Return redacted receipt data with PII obfuscated
        *   Discover necessary receptacle for data return
        *   Determine secure methods for connecting sources
        *   If necessary, develop connectors
    *   Go live plan
        *   Customer and Ryght shall agree to a project kickoff meeting and estimated project timeline document
        *   Ryght and Customer shall agree to a phased rollout to ensure accuracy and quality. Phase rollout considerations include, but are not limited to, volume of Receipts, various written languages beyond English.
