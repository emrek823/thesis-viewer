### Executive Summary

Abhishek Chandra presented Recora’s dramatic operational transformation through AI automation, achieving 28% gross margins currently with a target of 63% by year-end. The company has systematically replaced human labor across chart review (fired 30 people, reduced costs to 7%), benefits verification (down to 6% of previous costs using Stedi MCP + Silna AI agents), and front desk operations (voice agents handling patient communications). Chandra positions this as part of a broader industry shift where digital health companies can move from OpEx-heavy models to AI-enabled platforms that undercut traditional SaaS pricing. He sees massive consolidation opportunity in the $100M-$1B healthcare services market, advocating for direct MSO ownership rather than serving existing platforms like Healthie.
