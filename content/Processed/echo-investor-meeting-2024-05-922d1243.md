## Slide 3: Data Flows

# Data Flows

## Overall Process Flow

1.  **Child is diagnosed.** Provider Submits Pre-authorization Request (existing process)
2.  **Optum:** Pre-Auth Received.
3.  **Optum:** Informs Echo Of A New Autism Patient (via SFTP / API)
4.  **Optum (Provider side):**
    *   Caregiver enrolled in Echo and completes Caregiver Assessment
    *   Provider completes client assessment and transmits on Echo Provider Portal
    *   *\* Allows for targeted treatment plans*
5.  **Echo Clinical Screening:**
    *   Echo's proprietary AI insights + team of specialists review each assessment for progress / regression
6.  **Optum (Payer side):**
    *   Receives assessment including Echo's clinical team review on Echo payer portal
7.  **Optum (Optional Integration):**
    *   Optionally, Echo can integrate directly with Optum systems to transmit assessment (via API / SFTP)
8.  **Final Step:** Echo's clinical specialists makes recommendations to health plans to assist in claim decision.
