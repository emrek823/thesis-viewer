ZoomLogi, Inc., a Delaware Corporation

Job Title: CTO

Company Overview:
ZoomLogi, Inc. is on a mission to transform Visibility within Pharma logistics by preventing temperature-controlled logistics failures through real-time data aggregation, predictive recommendations, and rapid intervention. The Pharma supply chain is among the most demanding in logistics, and our software solution is purpose-built to meet these challenges head-on, providing visibility across temperature excursions, communication gaps, chain-of-custody failures, and more. As a member of our initial core team, you will play a pivotal role in achieving this vision, and work very closely with the founders.

Position Overview:
The Chief Technology Officer (CTO) will be responsible for building and scaling the company's technology stack, including the Platform UI, Driver App, and general infrastructure (front-end and back-end). This role will focus on developing and deploying scalable, user-centric solutions that enable real-time visibility, predictive insights, and seamless customer interactions. The CTO will collaborate closely with the Head of Integrations (and current interim CTO), as well as with other stakeholders to ensure technology aligns with business goals and customer needs.

Key Responsibilities:

Product Development & Platform Build-Out:

Oversee the development of Platform UI V1 (visibility, LSP scorecarding, location analytics) and V2 (import orders/shipments, sensor inventory, reporting).

Lead the design and implementation of the Driver App, enabling GPS tracking, checkpoint status confirmation, and communication (chat functionality).

Build in-platform communication features (e.g., calls, emails, texts) and integrate AI-powered check-call functionality (starting with 3rd-party integrations).

Drive the development of AI-based recommendation systems, starting with V1 (route updates) and expanding to V2 (provider/location optimization).

Scalable Infrastructure:

Ensure the platform is built to scale to 100+ users, 1000s of data point updates every 5-10mins in the next 24months

Ensure all systems and applications comply with data security, privacy, and other industry standards (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2, etc.).

Collaborate with the Head of Integrations to enhance data aggregation capabilities and build low-latency infrastructure for real-time platform updates.

Prioritize system stability, performance, and scalability to support rapid customer growth.

Team Building & Leadership:

Act as the lead engineer during the initial phases, writing code and delivering product versions.

Begin hiring and building an engineering team starting in June 2025, with additional hires in January 2026 and March 2026.

Leverage personal and network connections to source top engineering talent.

Recruit, train, and mentor new team members, fostering a culture of curiosity, scrappiness, and user-centric problem-solving.

Manage the technology budget, ensuring cost-effectiveness in tech stack, hiring, and infrastructure spending.

Problem-Solving & Innovation:

Drive user-centric innovation, iterating on the product suite based on customer feedback and emerging needs.

Focus on user engagement, system latency, and stability as key performance metrics.

Explore and adopt cutting-edge technologies to improve ZoomLogi’s tech stack and deliver differentiated value.

Stakeholder Collaboration:

Work in tandem with the Head of Integration & Analytics on all technical aspects

Work closely with customers, partners, and vendors to understand requirements, refine solutions, and ensure effective collaboration.

Report directly to the CEO, providing regular updates on progress, challenges, and innovations.

Qualifications:

Experience & Background:

Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.

Minimum of 8 years of experience in software engineering, or technical leadership roles.

Proven ability to build and scale software platforms in fast-paced startup environments.

Bonus points for experience in Healthcare, logistics, or related domains

Technical Skills:

Full stack, with experience dealing with real-time systems

Proficiency in front-end frameworks (e.g., Node.js, React and web socket development)

Familiar with stream processing for back-end (e.g., Kafka, Flink)

Experience with platform layer (e.g., Python, GoLang, Java)

Familiar with AI/ML models for recommendation systems or predictive analytics

Familiarity with cloud-based architecture, microservices, and scalable data pipelines.

Leadership:

Demonstrated ability to lead engineering teams, from initial hires to scaling.

Strong interpersonal and communication skills for cross-functional collaboration and stakeholder engagement.

Mindset & Attributes:

Entrepreneurial mindset with a scrappy, hands-on approach to problem-solving.

User-centric and data-driven, with a passion for improving product experience.

Thrives in ambiguity and fast-changing environments typical of early-stage startups.

Location:

This role is based in San Francisco, Los Angeles, or Chicago, and requires flexibility and willingness to travel.
