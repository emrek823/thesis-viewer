## Slide 3: Data Flows

### Data Flows

**Initial Process (existing process)**

1.  **Child is diagnosed.**
2.  **Provider Submits Pre-authorization Request** (existing process)
3.  **Optum:** Pre-Auth Received.
4.  **Optum:** Optum Informs Echo Of A New Autism Patient

**Echo Process Flow**

1.  **Optum:** Caregiver enrolled in Echo and completes Caregiver Assessment
    *   *Allows for targeted treatment plans*
2.  **Provider:** completes client assessment and transmits on Echo Provider Portal
    *   *(Data flow from Optum to Echo via SFTP, API)*
3.  **Echo Clinical Screening:** Echo's proprietary AI insights + team of specialists review each assessment for progress / regression
4.  **Optum:** receives assessment including Echo's clinical team review on Echo payer portal
    *   *(Data flow from Echo to Optum via API, SFTP)*
5.  **Optum:** Optionally, Echo can integrate directly with Optum systems to transmit assessment

**Footer Note:** Echo's clinical specialists makes recommendations to health plans to assist in claim decision.
