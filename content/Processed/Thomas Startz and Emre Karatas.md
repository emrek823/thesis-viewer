### Product Demo - Medical Necessity Review Agent

- ChatGPT-like interface for reviewing medical charts against payer criteria
	- Uploads clinical charts (3-page example: acute myocardial infarction)
	- Analyzes against standard criteria sets (InterQual or MCG)
	- Generates structured output with evidence and rationale
	- Serves both payer and provider users (nurses, physician advisors)
- Technical integration roadmap
	- Currently works with PDF uploads independent of EHR
	- Next 3 months: HL7 ADT messages, CCDA integration
	- Automatic prioritization of cases running nightly
- Broader applications beyond UM
	- Medical malpractice lawyer expressed interest at conference
	- Expandable to case management, quality programs
	- Foundation for any chart analysis workflow
