### AI Insurance Problem & Market Opportunity

- Insurance industry has lots of exclusions around AI, lots of fear, lacks data on how to underwrite AI risks
- Healthcare providers caught between wanting to accelerate AI adoption but doctors hold liability, innovation stalls
- Crisis of trust across AI - hospitals and companies not scaling AI beyond pilots and demos because of liability concerns
- Liability puzzle unclear: when AI kicks in, who is liable - doctor, provider, or hospital?
- Medical malpractice policy evolution still uncertain

### Product Vision & Approach

- Building AI risk management platform combining NIST AI framework, ISO 42001, AIX with observability layer
- Measuring AI risks from different levels within organization:
	- Governance and strategic level
	- How companies deploy AI
	- How systems are behaving day-to-day
- Three-phase vision:
	- AI risk management platform for providers to manage internal AI projects and vendor/partner risks
	- Build underwriting engine with proprietary data (not lawsuits/incidents data)
	- Add insurance layer on top based on proprietary data set
