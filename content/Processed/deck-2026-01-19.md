# Elera Health - Pre-Seed Pitch Deck 2026

## Overview
AI-enabled platform empowering outpatient providers to go independent.

## Problem
- **92M** people live in federally-designated primary care shortage areas (growing each year)
- **31 days** average wait to see an outpatient specialty provider
- **96%** of care is concentrated in clinic-based models, causing access barriers for millions
- Outpatient care can't be centralized: Primary and Specialty care are delivered in person and referrals are sourced locally
- Building a brand, operating a business, and delivering care at the same time is not sustainable for individual providers

## Why Now
- **27 states** now allow full practice authority for NPs without physician oversight (expanding to 34 this year)
- Most NPs cannot go independent due to complexity of branding, payer contracting, patient acquisition, and practice operations
- This **more than doubles** the outpatient independent market opportunity: from 500K addressable providers to north of 1M

## Solution
Elera turns licensed providers into in-network, hyper-local practices.

**Without Elera:**
- Only self-pay or confusing, long credentialing process
- Expensive marketing agencies or no brand at all
- Denials, long payment cycles, and complicated billing
- Spending hours scheduling and communicating
- Could take months to set up and start seeing patients

**With Elera:**
- Easy, fast credentialing with top national and local payers
- Professional website and online presence
- AI-powered RCM with guaranteed payments
- Admin tasks are automated in one comprehensive platform
- Providers start seeing patients within 3 weeks on average

## Flywheel
Integrated primary and specialty care strengthens access, referrals, outcomes, and economics.

- **Care Network**: Integrated primary and specialty practices delivering coordinated care across settings
- **Demand**: Shared patient demand and referrals flowing across the network
- **Outcomes**: Improved outcomes driven by coordination, data, and continuity of care
- **Economics**: Lower acquisition costs and higher lifetime value from internal referrals and network scale
