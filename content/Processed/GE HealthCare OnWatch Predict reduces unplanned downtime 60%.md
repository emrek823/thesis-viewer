# GE HealthCare OnWatch Predict

OnWatch Predict uses AI and digital twin technology for predictive maintenance of medical imaging equipment.

## Technology

- Uses healthcare IoT and AI learning on real-time equipment data
- Compares machine data against digital twin (virtual avatar)
- Creates unique virtual model based on AI learning
- Estimates remaining component life before disruption

## Results

### MRI Performance
- **60% reduction** in unplanned downtime
- **35% fewer** customer-initiated service requests
- **1,500 installations** in EMEA region

### CT Performance (2021 Study)
- 2,500 CT systems globally over 1 year
- **41% reduction** in unplanned downtime overall
- **75% reduction** in tube-related downtime (with TubeWatch)

## Service Model

When OnWatch Predict identifies potential failure:
- Advanced part delivery
- Proactive service scheduling
- Corrective action accommodates customer schedule
- Reduces unplanned medical equipment downtime

## Heritage

Draws on expertise of GE Aerospace engineers who use predictive remote assistance for commercial airliners.

## Future Expansion

Gradual transformation of service for all advanced imaging modalities: CT, nuclear medicine, ultrasound, digital radiography.
