Employers are facing the highest medical trend in over a decade, anticipating a staggering 9% increase in healthcare costs in 2026. What's more, Americans are not getting sicker. So, what's *really* driving these cost increases for organizations?

In this report, Garner's proprietary analysis of one of the largest claims dataset in the country identified four systemic drivers behind the healthcare cost crisis. Learn more about how top doctors use more cost effective sites of service, prescribe more effectively, and upcode at lower rates all to help protect employers from the very factors that are driving the cost crisis across the nation.
