# Page 4: Komodo has a validated advantage over legacy aggregators

## Komodo has a validated advantage over legacy aggregators

**Bar Chart: Total Patients on Therapy - Select Myeloma Therapies, Number of Patients by unique third-party token**

| Category          | Revlimid (35.2k total) | Pomalyst (10.7k total) | Darzalex (15.9k total) |
| :---------------- | :--------------------- | :--------------------- | :--------------------- |
| Komodo Unique     | 13.1K (37%)            | 3.7K (34%)             | 6.0K (38%)             |
| Dual Overlap      | 18.5K (53%)            | 5.9K (55%)             | 7.9K (49%)             |
| Aggregator Unique | 3.5K (10%)             | 1.2K (11%)             | 2.0K (13%)             |

**Key Insights:**
*   Using verifiable third-party tokens, Komodo can cross-reference its patient data to legacy aggregators.
*   Proprietary tokens make it hard to verify if patients are real or falsely presented as unique because they aren't generated from PII/PHI.
*   Komodo adds significant unique patients, largely derived from its proprietary relationships with payers.
*   Legacy aggregators have few additional lives (10-13% of total tokenized patients) on each drug.
