## Slide 5: 32H CLAIMSA - Claims Intake Automation
## 32H CLAIMSA - Claims Intake Automation

**02**
**32H**
**CLAIMSAI**

**32HEALTH.**

*   **Manual Claims Automation**
    Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) automation to convert paper claims (from scanning room, email, pdf, fax etc) into an electronic claim that can be used downstream for claim adjudication or further reviews by claim associates
*   **EDI Automation & Translation**
    Standardize intake of claims from multiple sources and formats (e.g: Portals, CSVs, Fax, Email, Paper) into EDI standards (e.g: 837, 834, 270/271, 276/277) for adjudicating claims in real-time with high degree of accuracy

### Claims Intake Automation Workflow
| Stage                                      | Description                                                                                                                                              |
| :----------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Claims Processes**                       | Claims submitted via provider portal, mailroom paper claims, faxes & emails                                                                              |
| **32H Claims AI / AI Confidence Score**    | Image is converted into digital with an AI Confidence Score                                                                                              |
| **Entity Resolution**                      | AI is used to resolve entity matching issues                                                                                                             |
| **Required Supporting Attachments**        | AI reviews the attachment to determine the right attachment was submitted                                                                                |
| **Resubmitted Claims for RFI & Appeals**   | AI is used to link claims to improve accuracy of claims handling and faster TAT                                                                          |
| **Utilization Review**                     | AI summarizes key clinical information submitted on claims and attachments reviewed by clinicians                                                        |

Reduce human intervention in claims workflow by 80%
