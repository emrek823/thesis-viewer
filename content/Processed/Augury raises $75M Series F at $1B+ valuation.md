# Augury Raises $75M Series F at $1B+ Valuation

Augury announced $75M Series F funding maintaining $1B+ valuation to accelerate industrial AI solutions.

## Funding Details

- **Amount:** $75M Series F
- **Valuation:** $1B+ (maintained unicorn status)
- **Total Raised:** $361M to date
- **Lead Investor:** Lightrock
- **Participants:** Insight Partners, Eclipse, Qumra Capital, Schneider Electric Ventures, Qualcomm Ventures

## Growth Metrics

Since 2021 funding round:
- **5x revenue increase**
- **3x customer base** among Fortune 500 manufacturers
- **3x increase** in $1M+ accounts
- **500M+ hours** of machine data analyzed
- **$1B+ value** generated for customers in 40+ countries

## Technology Performance

- **99.9% failure detection accuracy**
- **5-20x ROI** when deployed at scale
- Predictive maintenance for industrial equipment
- Process optimization via AI
