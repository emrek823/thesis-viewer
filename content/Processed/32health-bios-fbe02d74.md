# Shaju Puthuserry
## CEO

Shaju is Chief Executive Officer for 32Health, with a vision to provide the richest dental and vision benefits at the lowest cost resulting in the best patient outcome and experiences through an AI/NLP powered modern dental & vision insurance administrative platform. Most recently, Shaju was the COO at Overjet, where he successfully led the company to a post series B valuation of $433M and partnered with top dental insurance companies and dental clinics to realize value and outcomes.

Shaju holds a master's degree in theoretical physics from Boston College. With over two decades of experience in technology, business, and health care, Shaju has held several senior level leadership positions in dental space as Chief Analytics Officer at DentaQuest, Chief Information Officer at P&R Dental Strategies, and CTO & co-founder of his own technology firm at P&R Technologies.

He is a frequent speaker at payer and provider communities and health care work groups.

# Deepak Ramaswamy
## CTO

Deepak is Chief Technology Officer for 32Health and is responsible for the technology vision and oversees the entire product life cycle including the technology infrastructure, AI/NLP machine learning and engineering development. Most recently, Deepak was CTO & Co-Founder at Overjet, where he successfully led the company to a post series B valuation of $433M and built the engineering, machine learning and infosec teams and near real time systems for performing AI inferences. Deepak has deep expertise in the dental AI and dental insurance space.

Deepak has more than 20+ years of experience in the computational technology and start-up space, with two successful exits - acquisitions by Ansoft, Ansys. In addition, Deepak has strong product management & engineering leadership experiences at Amazon & Interactive Brokers.

Deepak holds a PHD in Information Technology from MIT, MBA from Cornell University and BTech from IIT, Kharagpur. Outside of work, Deepak spends his time with his family and mentoring alumni for careers in machine learning and software engineering.

# Dr. Robert Faiella DMD
## CDO

Dr. Faiella is Chief Dental Officer for 32Health. Dr. Faiella's demonstrated business and leadership experience, along with his capabilities as a dental specialist, uniquely positions him to provide clinical leadership at 32Health.

Dr. Faiella's 35-year career has been focused on providing quality patient care and serving and improving the dental profession. He is a Past President of the American Dental Association, and former Chairman and President of ADA Business Enterprises, Inc. He currently serves as President and Director of the American Board of Periodontology, Vice President for the American College of Dentists, Chair of the Harvard School of Dental Medicine Board of Fellows, and appointed member of the MIT Sloan School of Management Alumni Board. In addition, he is Chair of the ADA Standards Committee on Dental Informatics Working Group 13.8 on Artificial and Augmented Intelligence for the development of standards and technical reports under the American National Standards Institute.

Dr. Faiella received his D.M.D degree from Fairleigh Dickinson University School of Dental Medicine, M.M.Sc. from Harvard Medical School, and his postdoctoral NIH Fellowship in Periodontology at Harvard School of Dental Medicine, where he has also served as a faculty member. He also received his M.B.A. at MIT Sloan School of Management. He has received the Distinguished Alumnus Award from the Harvard School of Dental Medicine, and has delivered the Commencement Address to eight Schools of Dental Medicine over the past fourteen years.

# Ronald Inge, DDS
## CSO

Dr. Ron Inge is the Chief Strategy Officer for 32Health. In this role, he brings to bear his years of experience in the dental benefits industry to assist in the development of artificial intelligence models to perform routine administrative tasks. His insight into these processes allows the 32Health platform to create efficiencies and reduce the cost to administer dental benefits.

Dr. Ron Inge was the chief dental officer, chief operating officer and vice president of professional services at Delta Dental of Missouri. He joined the organization in 2016. As chief dental officer and vice president of professional services, Dr. Inge was responsible for all aspects of the dental networks, serving as the primary liaison with dental professionals, as well as setting and implementing dental policy and procedures. He also coordinated consumer-focused oral health initiatives and assisted in product development. As chief operating officer, he directed the organization's operations, including the claims and customer service teams.

Dr. Inge spent the first 15 years of his career in private dental practice. He has served in executive roles at Aetna, Delta Dental of Washington, Delta Dental of California, the American Dental Association, DentiCare of California and AVP Vision Plans. His experience also includes serving as chief dental officer for Western Dental Services, a large dental service organization, in Orange, California.

Dr. Inge was involved in the original concept and development of the Institute for Oral Health (IOH) in 2006 and served as the organization's executive director for more than eight years. He served on the boards of the National Association of Dental Plans and the California Association of Dental Plans. Dr. Inge is also a member of The Santa Fe Group, which fosters policies and initiates actions to improve oral health, overall health and the well-being of the public. He received his Bachelor of Arts degree in human biology from Stanford University and his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of California, Los Angeles.

# Kristen Strasheim
## VP Product

Kristen is the VP of Product. She focuses on 32Health's dental and vision products life cycle and driving high-level product initiatives to support overall business goals for all partners.

Kristen has been a registered dental hygienist for over 30 years. She has a distinctive, and extensive dental knowledge in private practice, public health, and the dental insurance industry. She has almost 18 years of experience working at a leading dental insurance company. She was responsible for establishing, implementing and maintaining all clinical dental aspects for the Group Division Dental Insurance product, accountable for all aspects of the Utilization Review and Utilization Management Programs including the responsibility for ensuring compliance with state, federal and URAC standards. She educated dental professionals across the United States regarding dental insurance. She led the project to obtain URAC accreditation in Health Utilization Management which resulted in the first dental insurance company to achieve this designation and maintained the accreditation for 15 years.

Kristen managed the State of Nebraska Health and Human Services System, Dental Health Division. She has didactic and clinical teaching experience with dental hygiene and dental students at various colleges in Nebraska and Iowa and she was a contributor to a textbook for dental students on dental practice management. She has an extensive history of involvement with the American Dental Association, all of the dental specialty organizations, and National Association of Dental Plans work groups as vice-chair of the Current Dental Terminology, vice-chair of the Diagnostic Terminology, and Clinical Quality Measure Work Group.

Kristen received her Bachelors of Science in Dental Hygiene from the University Missouri Kansas City. She is passionate about dentistry and as a result, she was the recipient of the Nebraska Dental Public Health Award and Dental Hygiene Advocate of the Year. Outside of work, Kristen and her family rescues large breed dogs with Big Dogs Huge Paws.
