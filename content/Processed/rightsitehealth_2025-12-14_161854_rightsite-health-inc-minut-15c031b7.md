**Proceedings:**
A meeting of the Board of Directors (the “Board”) was called to order at 2:00 p.m. CT by Jamo Rubin. There was a sufficient number of Board Members present to constitute a quorum. The present board members approved the minutes from the prior meeting.

Jamo began the meeting by reviewing September and Q325 financial and operational results. He then presented Q425 priorities.

Clayton reviewed key payor activities and EMS agency updates. The Board asked questions and engaged in discussion on these topics. Jamo briefly reviewed progress with the walk-in service at University Health as an extension of the sales update.

Lastly, Jamo reviewed Care Team operations. Participants asked questions and engaged in discussion about these initiatives.

**The meeting was adjourned at 3:00 p.m. CT.**
