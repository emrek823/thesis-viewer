About Event

​Standard Model Bio is teaming up with TechBio Transformers to host a happy hour in Palo Alto while our founders are in town for JPM. We've thoughtfully curated an guest list of scientists, engineers, and clinicians who work at the intersection of AI and biomedicine. Please join us for an evening of great food, drinks, and conversation. Appetizers, tacos, beer, and Sangria will be provided.

​About [Standard Model Bio](https://www.standardmodel.bio/) — We build multimodal foundation models that integrate any biomedical measurement at any scale, representing patient state and predicting the next clinical event. We partner with biopharma and collaborate with investigators at academic medical centers, powering clinical trials and clinical decision support. Our team has expertise across tech and biopharma and we are backed by Virtue and Arkitekt Ventures.

​About [TechBio Transformers](https://linktr.ee/techbiotransformers) — TBT is a community for thoughtful nerds and passionate professionals who work on the world's most important problems at the intersection of biology, software, and AI. If you are a scientist, product manager, computational biologist, academic, or engineer, this is your 3rd place. It's where we hope you find meaningful conversations, collaborate on projects and find friends that work on the same problems as you, pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.
