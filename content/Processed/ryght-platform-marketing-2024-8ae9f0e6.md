## Slide 17: Competitive Positioning

# Competitive Positioning

*   Companies like Certara, Huma.ai, and Ferma are merely black box companies.
*   Ryght's customers want to be able to interrogate their own internal data and documentation via a system that understands the language of life science, and with security and privacy as non-negotiable requirements.
*   Ryght differentiates from these competitors with its flexible platform architecture that allows our customers to leverage advanced GenAI-based insights both to internal teams and to their clients through embeddable application endpoints.
*   This approach accelerates our clients software development efforts and gives them a path to deliver value to their clients faster.
