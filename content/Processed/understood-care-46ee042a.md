## Slide 6: Patient's journey to find medical transportation independently

**Title:** Patient's journey to find medical transportation independently
**Subtitle:** Medical transportation is a common problem we help with

1.  Patient cannot get to their doctor appointment because they cannot drive.
2.  Patient researches different forms of no or low cost medical transportation offered by their insurance, county and provider offices.
3.  Patient applies for each type medical transportation individually and fills out multiple long forms.
4.  Patient coordinates with their PCP for letter(s) of medical necessity.
5.  Patient schedules an interview with the payer or county to confirm medical need.
6.  Patient emails and calls to nudge their application along.

Patient gets approved and has a ride to their appointment.

## Slide 7: Patient's journey to get medical transportation with an Advocate

**Title:** Patient's journey to get medical transportation with an Advocate

1.  Patient cannot get to their doctor appointment because they cannot drive.
2.  Patient signs up for Understood Care. Patient is matched with an MD for an intake and assigned to an advocate.
3.  Patient speaks to their advocate about transportation. The advocate researches and leads the application process.

Patient gets approved and has a ride to their appointment.
