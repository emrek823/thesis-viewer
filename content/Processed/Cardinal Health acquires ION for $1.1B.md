# Cardinal Health Acquires Integrated Oncology Network for $1.1B

Cardinal Health completed acquisition of ION for $1.115B cash.

## Transaction Details

- **Price:** $1.115B cash
- **Announced:** September 20, 2024
- **Closed:** December 2, 2024

## About ION

- Management services organization
- Supports 50+ practice sites in 10 states
- 100+ providers
- Continuum of care: medical oncology, radiation oncology, urology, ancillary services

## Integration

ION integrated into Navista, Cardinal Health's managed services organization. Practices gain access to:
- AI algorithms for identifying high-risk patients
- Clinical trial matching for cancer patients
- Integrated practice management and EHR platform

## Financial Impact

Expected to be accretive to non-GAAP EPS after 12 months. Results reported within Pharma segment.

## Regulatory

No FTC intervention - another regulatory green light for MSO consolidation in oncology.
