# U-ST HealthProof

## Our Strategic Partnerships

Strategic alliances and collaboration with industry pioneers of healthcare technology allow us to leverage collective expertise as we team up to shape the future of healthcare.

Our ecosystem brings together our proprietary technology with partner solutions that keep us on the leading edge of AI, technology advances, and improved capabilities.

## Key Partners

### Core Administrative Processing System (CAPS)

**HealthEdge**
HealthEdge is working to usher in the era of the digital payer by providing a digital foundation for healthcare payers. We offer an advanced CAPS suite that can provide you with the efficiency, flexibility, insights, and agility necessary to control costs, embrace change, and move quickly to take advantage of new opportunities.

### Portals/Shared Experience Platform

**HealthTrio**
An engaging online experience and a robust set of self-service tools is the new standard for healthcare organizations across the country. Using these platforms, we truly drive the triple aim of reduced cost, better outcomes, and an improved experience.

### Care Management

**HealthEdge**
Our solution suite provides easy-to-use, next-generation data integration and workflow management tools through a system that helps you operationalize an evidence-based, person-centered Care Management strategy for your populations.

### Interoperability

**HealthTrio**
We embrace the advancing use of APIs. Our solution is built upon highly interchangeable mechanisms for data consumption and sharing, creating a great opportunity to leverage alternative connectivity to the health plan's internal administrative systems and those of their partners while meeting regulatory requirements.

### Provider Management

**Santech**
We offer comprehensive solutions to transform Provider Data and Network Management. We ensure that payers and healthcare organizations can trust in the precision and reliability of their data, allowing them to drive network agility and focus on delivering high-quality care.

### EDI Gateway/Clearinghouse

**Edifecs**
We work to optimize end-to-end patient data exchange and derive insights for our healthcare customers so they can better serve their members and patients.

**Smart Data Solutions**
Our solution allows for seamless data exchange between health plans and provider networks. Data, AI, and automation are the foundational layer for our products and solutions.

### Payment Integrity

**HealthEdge**
We offer payers true Payment Accountability® with the ability to unlock transformation at the reimbursement, payment integrity, and enterprise level. As a complete solution, clients can achieve Enterprise Transformation, where root-cause issues are identified and addressed upstream, and all aspects of claims operations are centralized for comprehensive business intelligence.

**Zelis**
We're passionate about modernizing the healthcare financial experience for all, aligning the interests of payers, providers, and healthcare consumers. Manage all payments and communications in one intelligent hub, unlocking efficiencies, ensuring compliance, and delivering the modern and customized payments experience that providers need and members demand – now, and in the future.

### Mailroom

**Smart Data Solutions**
Data, AI and automation are the foundational layer for our products and solutions. With exceptional capabilities in intelligent document management, workflow, and connectivity, we offer a variety of products and solutions to address your unique needs.

### Print Fulfillment

**Zelis**
Communications are a key component to a modern and positive healthcare financial experience, for providers and members. We've developed a proprietary platform to simplify design and streamline distribution to ensure your members have the information they need when they need it. It takes data from multiple sources and ensures every piece of communication – from ID cards to EOBs - includes the most accurate information.

### Revenue Integrity

**Edifecs**
Edifecs Encounter Submissions is a best-in-class SaaS-based solution that utilizes AI-enabled coding and analytics to break down encounter operations silos, support submission accuracy, and ensure compliance across all managed care lines of business.

### Digital Service Center/Engagement

**Genesys**
We provide holistic support to patients and members with digital tools and multichannel experiences. Let them manage their wellness at the most convenient time and channel with the best resource.

## About HealthProof

Founded in 2016, we are the most trusted, valued partner for health plans and other risk bearing providers. We offer a modern integrated ecosystem of healthcare operations, processes, and products, with inherent scalability, efficiency, and predictable outcomes. Our BPaaS delivery solutions work behind the scenes to manage our customers' complex admin operations, giving them elbow room to focus on their members' needs and well-being.

Bending cost curves, guaranteeing outcomes, finding paths through roadblocks, that's our way of life. Our customers count on us to safely navigate them through deadlocks. We operate as an umbrella organization, with our three powerhouses: HealthProof, Advalent, and Advantasure and have a strong global presence, with our workforce of 4000+ people spread across the world.
