# Sword Health Raises $130M at $3B Valuation

AI-powered virtual physical therapy platform Sword Health raised funding boosting valuation to $3B (50% increase from $2B in 2021).

## Funding Details

- **Amount:** $130M (mix of primary and secondary)
- **Secondary:** $100M allowing employee liquidity
- **Total Raised:** $340M to date
- **Lead Investors:** Khosla Ventures, General Catalyst, BOND, Founders Fund

## Company Performance

- Nearly tripled revenue in prior year
- Strong cash position, approaching profitability
- CEO hinted at potential 2025 IPO

## Product: Phoenix AI Care Specialist

Alongside funding, launched Phoenix - first AI Care Specialist that guides members during sessions through natural conversation. Starting with Thrive (physical pain), then Bloom (women's pelvic health).

## Outcome-Based Pricing

Sword pioneered outcome-based pricing: 50% on activation, 50% on goal achievement. This model reduces employer risk and aligns incentives.
