## Slide 4: Consequences of Lack of Intervention

## Slide 6: Flourish Is a Data-Driven Early Intervention Platform
Flourish Is a Data-Driven Early Intervention Platform

## Slide 7: Outcomes of Early Intervention
Outcomes of Early Intervention

| Condition                 | Reduction Percentage | Description                                                                      |
| :------------------------ | :------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **PSYCHOSIS RELATED CONDITIONS** | 86%                  | Reduction in hospitalizations, with employment rates doubling within 6 months.   |
| **SIGNIFICANT MOOD DISORDERS** | 40%                  | Reduction in hospitalizations, with a 56% reduction in overall relapses.        |
| **SIGNIFICANT BEHAVIORAL DISORDERS** | 58%                  | Reduction in overall healthcare utilization, with a 64% reduction in out of home placements. |
| **RISKY SUBSTANCE USE**   | 53%                  | Reduction in substance use, with adolescents being 6X less likely to be arrested. |
