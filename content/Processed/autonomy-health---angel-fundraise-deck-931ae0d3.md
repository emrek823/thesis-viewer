## Slide 3: PROBLEM

**Private practice neurology is under pressure yet there's a deep desire to remain independent**

*   **Declining Reimbursement**
    *   Rates are down ~10% since 2020 and denials up to 17%
*   **Increasing Costs**
    *   Inflation has increased ~22% over the same period
*   **Staffing Challenges**
    *   Wage increases coupled with staff turnover creates pressure

**Quotes:**

"We equally recognize the passion you both have for the vision of keeping neurology practices independent and it's perfectly aligned with our strategies."
– CEO, Outpatient Neurology Practice

"I have wanted to go on my own for many years, but have been too worried about taking the risk. I feel like under your guidance I could make this transition."
– Neurology Nurse Practitioner

## Slide 7: MVP

*   Botox PAs are unique to neurology
