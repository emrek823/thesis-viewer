### Community Paramedicine Model

- Concept created by fire chiefs 25 years ago to manage rising 911 call volumes
- Proven effectiveness: reduces 911 calls among frequent flyers by up to 80%
- Historical gap: fire/EMS only connected to healthcare at ED drop-off point
- Current fragmentation: hundreds of individual contracts needed between health plans and EMS agencies
- Paralign solution: first clinically integrated network (CIN) for EMS agencies
	- Single contract structure eliminates need for hundreds of separate agreements
	- Platform enables scaling to thousands of lives

### Financial Model & Performance Metrics

- Value-based care from outset (fixed fee model rejected by health plans)
- Revenue structure:
	- Pass-through fees to EMS partners (majority of revenue)
	- Paralign compensation tied to performance pool
- Performance measures (weighted differently):
	- Data capture: HEDIS measures and care gap closure
	- Hit rate: outperform health plan case management teams
	- Utilization: reduce ED visits and hospital days
	- Cost reduction: overall care cost decrease
- Targeting 40% margin per member by years 3-4
- Negotiating advanced PMPM payments with annual true-up

### EMS Agency Economics
