## Slide 7: We Offer a "True" Guarantee. Other Early Entrants Are Limited
PRODUCT OVERVIEW

What is included and excluded in your PMPM Guarantee? It often varies

**Some Exclude All Specialty**
* Only covers brand and generic drugs
* Lowest value to Plan Sponsor
* Unable to use as a replacement for "expected Rx spend"

**Some Exclude Drugs over $ Threshold**
* Includes specialty (and all drugs) up to a specified $ amount
* Medium-High value to Plan Sponsor
* Can be a meaningful benchmark for budgeting

**WriteWise: Limited Exclusions**
* Covers all drugs, with few exceptions
* Highest value to Plan Sponsor
* Can be used by Plan Sponsor as a true budget

## Slide 8: Reconciling a PMPM Guarantee
PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Simplified unit of measurement and comparison for Plan Sponsors.

**Guarantee**
The guarantee is negotiated using the WriteWise platform before the first year of a PBM contract.

**Monitor**
WriteWise monitors the plan performance throughout the year with a data feed integrated directly into the PBM.

**Audit**
WriteWise will audit the plan performance at the end of the contract year.

**Reconcile**
**Underperformance:** The PBM will be required to reimburse the Plan Sponsor in accordance with the guarantee.
**Overperformance:** The PBM may have an opportunity to share in savings
