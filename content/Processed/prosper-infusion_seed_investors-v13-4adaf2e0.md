## Slide 6: Why Home? Home Infusion is ~50% Cheaper than Hospital-Based Care

### Infusion Monthly Costs for Top 5 Conditions¹

| Condition             | Hospital Infusion | Home Infusion |
| :-------------------- | :---------------- | :------------ |
| Multiple Sclerosis    | $21,000           | $11,000       |
| Immune Deficiency     | $12,000           | $7,000        |
| Rheumatoid Arthritis  | $11,000           | $6,000        |
| Inflammatory Bowel Disease | $11,000           | $7,000        |
| Chemotherapy          | $12,000           | $8,000        |

The top 5 conditions account for 75% of total private insurance spending for infusion¹

Switching infusion from the hospital to the home would save $16K-$37K / yr¹

1 - Administering Specialty Drugs Outside Hospitals Can Improve Care and Reduce Costs by $4 Billion Each Year, UnitedHealth Group

## Slide 7: How Prosper Infusion Care Delivery Works

1.  Schedule patients who needs IV therapy from hospital, physician, or plan
2.  Verify patient eligibility, financial assistance, prior authorization
3.  Compound drug, ship to patient, send home infusion nurse, identify care gaps
4.  Bill insurance, coordinate with specialist, and remotely address care gaps
