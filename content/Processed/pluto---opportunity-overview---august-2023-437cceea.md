## Slide 9: market size & opportunity

**Map Information:**
*   Core geographic focus, including NY, NJ, CT, PA, and FL markets.
*   Secondary geographic focus, including Midwest regional markets.

**Large & Growing Addressable Market**
Home Health market is expected to grow 90% from $130 bn (2021) to $250 bn (2030) at a 7.5% CAGR.

**Fragmented Provider Base**
43,000 Home Health providers in the US.
11,300 Medicare-certified agencies, of which 92% have annual revenues less than $5m.

**Secular Tailwinds**
US population aged 65+ projected to grow from 56 mm (2021, 15% of total) to 73 mm (2030, 20%).
Regulatory changes (Value-based care, Medicare Advantage) will clean up waste in the at-home care system.

**Underserved Market**
Healthcare services market is underfunded and underbanked, verified by discussions with providers.
