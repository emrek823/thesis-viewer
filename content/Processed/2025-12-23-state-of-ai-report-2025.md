Now in its eighth year, the **State of AI Report 2025** is reviewed by leading AI practitioners in industry and research. It considers the following key dimensions, including a new large-scale AI usage survey section:1. **Research:** Technology breakthroughs and their capabilities.
2. **Industry:** Areas of commercial application for AI and its business impact.
3. **Politics:** Regulation of AI, its economic implications and the evolving geopolitics of AI.
4. **Safety:** Identifying and mitigating catastrophic risks that highly-capable future AI systems could pose to us.
5. **Survey:** The largest open-access survey of 1,200 AI practitioners and their AI usage patterns. You can  **[participate here](https://www.stateof.ai/survey)**.
6. **Predictions:** What we believe will happen in the next 12 months and a performance review of last year's predictions to keep us honest.
