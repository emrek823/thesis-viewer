BioArena is a community-driven platform that benchmarks biomedical AI models through anonymous, head-to-head comparisons on real biomedical prompts. Domain experts vote on the most accurate and scientifically sound answers, producing transparent rankings based on real-world biomedical performance, not static test sets.  
  
Inspired by [MedArena.ai](http://medarena.ai/) ([James Zou](https://www.linkedin.com/in/james-zou-2123a4133/)) and following the path pioneered by [llmarena.ai](http://llmarena.ai/), BioArena is built by and for the biomedical research community.  
That‚Äôs why access is via [Synapse.org](http://synapse.org/) a large online biomedical community -- and why we‚Äôll continue adding safeguards to ensure questions and ratings remain deeply biomedical in focus.

Still, imperfect evaluation beats no evaluation, especially when it is open, iterative, and community-governed.
