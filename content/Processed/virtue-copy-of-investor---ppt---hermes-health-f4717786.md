# The Problem: High Overhead to Request Records

The Many to Many Network leads Requestors to suffer from:

*   **High Overhead:** Requestors have to physically call and fax, providers to obtain medical records

*   **Stringent Compliance Requirements:** Systems and processes must continuously adapt to evolving security and regulatory standards

## Slide 32: Provider Fees

# Provider Fees

**Providers Charge**

*   Provider fees are fees charged by the provider to retrieve the Record.
    *   Rates are limited by federal and state regulations
*   Provider fees are in addition to the fees to Request a Record.
*   Hermes Health passes through Provider fees back to its clients

**Opportunity**

*   Hermes Health will negotiate bulk pricing with hospital systems, EHRs, and ROI vendors that averages the cost of Provider fees
*   This allows Hermes Health to offer flat fee pricing back to its customers reducing pricing volatility and de-risking project costs for clients
*   Hermes Health can then take the margin (the difference between average costs and the flat fee cost).
