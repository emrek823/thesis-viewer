### Market Trends & Investment Sentiment

- Investor confidence remains uncertain on software investments, particularly around 2-year ROI visibility
- Emerging sentiment shift toward AI-enabled care delivery models as potentially safer investment area
- Healthcare AI moving from hype to real execution phase
	- Point solution fatigue growing among customers/buyers
	- More ROI slides showing actual FTE reductions and profit improvements
	- AI adoption crossing chasm - becoming “must have” vs nice-to-have

### Technology & Competitive Landscape

- Snowflake Cortex disrupting application layer
	- Series B+ companies using it to DIY LLM implementations
	- Replacing Pinecone/LangChain for orchestration
	- Easy integration with existing analytics flows
	- Threat to application layer companies lacking strong guardrails/safety models
- EMR vendors striking back
	- Athena launching free ambient scribe to compete with Abridge/Ambiance
	- New CDI competitor to Naveena, releasing HCC by HCC
	- Payer integration features bringing prior auth logic to point of care
- Children’s Hospital Atlanta using Ember for real-time payer rule application in EMR
- Multiple “Turquoise Health successors” emerging (Third Wave mentioned)
	- Enriching MRF data to calculate actual allowable amounts
