# Hinge Health S-1 IPO Filing

Digital physical therapy company Hinge Health filed for IPO and began trading on NYSE under ticker "HNGE" at $32/share.

## Key Financials (2024)

- **Revenue:** $390.4M (33% YoY growth from $292.7M)
- **Gross Profit:** $299.9M (77% gross margin, up from 66%)
- **Net Loss:** $11.9M (narrowed from $108.1M in 2023)
- **Free Cash Flow:** $45M positive
- **IPO Valuation:** $2.6-2.9B (down 60% from $6.2B in 2021)

## Q1 2025 Performance

- **Net Profit:** $17.1M (vs $26.5M loss in Q1 2024)
- First profitable quarter

## Business Metrics

- **Members:** 532,000+ (up from 371,000)
- **Clients:** 2,250+ (up from 1,650)
- **Fortune 100:** Nearly half are clients
- **Primary customers:** Self-insured employers, public sector, labor unions

## Market Significance

Industry observers view this as the IPO that could kickstart digital health IPO market revival.
