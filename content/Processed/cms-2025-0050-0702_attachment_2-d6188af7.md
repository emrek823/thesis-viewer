## Slide 12: HealthEx supports a wide range of high value use cases

HealthEx

**HealthEx supports a wide range of high value use cases**

*   **Value based care & quality initiatives**
    Access real-time patient data to enhance care coordination, medication adherence, population health initiatives, and other quality initiatives like HEIDS or STAR measurements

*   **Prior auth, referral, risk adjustment, and claims data support**
    Access real-time patient data to improve prior auth and referral management turnaround times, and enable improved risk adjustment and claims data approval rates

*   **Consent-aware enforcement tools**
    Ensure patient data is accessed compliantly and can be utilized at scale, by providing consent-aware enforcement tools and customized data policy engine support across your system. Includes support for CMS-0057-F, clinical research, data privacy & more

*   **Interoperable data access**
    Enable data access via TEFCA-aligned infrastructure, for a unified, real-time view of patient data including prescriptions, encounters, and lab results across care sites
