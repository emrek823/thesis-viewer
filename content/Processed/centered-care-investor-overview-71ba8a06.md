## Slide 3: Centered Care is a healthcare data and analytics platform for care in senior living.

* * * * * * * * * Centered Care is a healthcare data and analytics platform for care in senior living.
We partner with senior living operators and care providers to transform senior living into care-focused communities centered on resident health and well-being.
We join compassionate service with leading-edge technology to enable personalized, high quality, goal-aligned care in the communities that seniors trust and love.
