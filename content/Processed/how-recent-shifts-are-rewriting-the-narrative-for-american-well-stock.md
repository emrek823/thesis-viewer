What's in the News

American Well issued new Q4 2025 revenue guidance of $51M to $54M, signaling expectations for continued sequential growth into year end.

The company trimmed its full year 2025 revenue outlook to a range of $245M to $248M from a prior $245M to $250M, reflecting a slightly more cautious view on near term demand.

Management emphasized that the updated outlook represents a narrowing of guidance rather than a reset, indicating that the broader 2025 revenue framework remains intact.

Taken together, the guidance changes point to a company focused on balancing growth with visibility, as it fine tunes expectations while reiterating confidence in its underlying trajectory.

How This Changes the Fair Value For American Well

Fair Value: Unchanged at 8.13x, indicating no revision to the intrinsic value estimate despite other model adjustments.

Discount Rate: Risen slightly from 8.38% to approximately 8.42%, reflecting a modest increase in perceived risk or funding costs.

Revenue Growth: Fallen slightly, with the long term growth assumption reduced from about -2.00% to roughly -2.07%.

Net Profit Margin: Effectively unchanged at around 7.11%, suggesting stable long term profitability expectations.

Future P/E: Risen marginally from about 10.51x to approximately 10.54x, signaling a slightly higher assumed valuation multiple on future earnings.
