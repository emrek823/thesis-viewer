## Splitting the healthcare company into stand-alone pharmacy or health-insurance businesses could leave each struggling

By

Updated ET

[CVS Health](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/CVS) [CVS 2.93% increase; green up pointing triangle](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/CVS) has spent more than $88 billion in the past half-dozen years to add a major health insurer, a clinic operator and a home-visit provider to its namesake pharmacies.

Now, the healthcare company [might break it all up](https://www.wsj.com/health/healthcare/cvs-board-conducts-strategic-review-of-company-81dc996f?mod=article_inline).
