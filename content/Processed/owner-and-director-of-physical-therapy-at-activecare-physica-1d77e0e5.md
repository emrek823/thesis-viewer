# Silna Health - Owner and Director of Physical Therapy at ActiveCare Physical Therapy

## Summary
A physical therapist and private practice owner shared their experience with Silna for insurance verification but eventually churned due to manual data entry issues and changes in service terms. They switched back to using Prompt EMR's pVerify feature for real-time benefits checks, believing that Prompt EMR's integrated solutions, like OnusOne for payroll, are more beneficial. The expert emphasized the importance of EMRs expanding product offerings to stand out in the market. They also discussed challenges in healthcare, such as insurance verification bottlenecks and the need for complete plan benefits information. The expert plans to adopt AI technology for documentation, including PredictionHealth integrated with Prompt, to improve compliance and note quality. They prefer using PredictionHealth and Sidekick over other tools in the market.
