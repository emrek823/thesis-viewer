## Slide 9: Nov - Customer conversation updates

**Nov - Customer conversation updates**
Overview: 30 customer prospect convos logged

**Highlights**
1.  **Late-stage funnel momentum:**
    Progress Pharma signed (Specialty Pharma, 10k/mo shipments potential), Verily & Minaris CGT soon. Catalent is actively testing the sandbox with an IT-director decision next. Cullinan is lining up a Q1 pilot for high-value cold-chain programs.
2.  **AI voice + white-label story resonating across segments:**
    Thermo Fisher, Sanofi Ventures, SSM Health, Polaris Rx, Arvato, Cencora, Nestlé Health Science and others all called out the AI calling + white-label tracking links as differentiated—describing the agent as realistic and “definitely helpful”—and tied it to concrete use cases (temperature excursions, LTC nursing-home runs, mail-order/specialty pharmacy, OPO ground transports).
3.  **Strengthened enterprise pipeline:**
    Deepening conversations with strategic logos and multipliers: Sanofi Ventures (investment + customer path), Pfizer clinical supply, Cencora’s $1B supply-chain program, Arvato’s European healthcare network

**Lowlight**
1.  **Enterprise decision latency & prioritization drag:**
    Cencora, Polaris Rx, SSM Health, Pfizer and Sanofi all see strong fit but are juggling crowded roadmaps (e.g., Polaris has ~90 IT projects with only top 5 funded, Sanofi Ventures on a Q2’26 timeline, Cencora still organizing data on Databricks), so many of the biggest opportunities remain medium-to long-term.

**Key quotes**
*   "The AI temperature-alert call was definitely helpful." Thermo Fisher (Ray)
*   "Those 24-48-hour therapies where all hell breaks loose are exactly where this makes sense.” Sanofi Ventures (Alba)
*   "We're data rich... but it's not organized in a way that's easiest to use to make critical business decisions." Cencora (Brent)
*   "I want the nursing-home directors to have a Domino's Pizza Tracker for their deliveries." Polaris Rx (CTO Matt Pollock)
*   "I feel fairly confident we do not have something this sophisticated." Nestlé Health Science / Aimmune (Nirav)
*   "People are going to be willing to pay more money to make sure their samples get there.” Gig Labs / ARUP (Vincent)
