## Slide 20: employee benefits today

confidential
June 2024
20

employee benefits today

### Employees
*   Limited medical choices (one to three)
*   Very limited ancillary and voluntary choices with one option for each category (i.e. mental health)
*   Limited or no guidance on benefit selection
*   Not portable (lose with change in employment)

### Employers
*   Manage broker/TPA relationship
*   Investigate, select and manage benefit offerings and providers from 1,000s of options (typically three to 100)
*   Work with their BenAdmin to “install” the benefits and run “open enrollment”
*   Reconcile and pay each benefit provider
*   Field employee issues and complaints
*   Encounter significant YoY increases in cost
*   File compliance reports
