## Slide 8: TAM - Vertical market expansion

We can use AI in ASO and risk markets will return ~20x revenue in the coming years and transform insurance from a service business to tech business

### Market Size (Concentric Circles)
*   **Dental Insurance Premium Market:** $110B
*   **Third Party Admin Services:** $18B
*   **SaaS for dental administration:** $6B

### Strategy
1.  We start here by providing SaaS for dental insurance administration for both payers and tpas
2.  We disrupt the TPA market by reducing admin fees from 17% to 5%
3.  Capture insurance market by offering the best product to the patients at lowest prices than incumbents
