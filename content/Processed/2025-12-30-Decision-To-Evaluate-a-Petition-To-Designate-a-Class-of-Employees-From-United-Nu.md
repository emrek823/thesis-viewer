## Abstract
NIOSH gives notice of a decision to evaluate a petition to designate a class of employees from the United Nuclear Corporation in Hematite, Missouri, as an addition to the Special Exposure Cohort (SEC) under the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000.
