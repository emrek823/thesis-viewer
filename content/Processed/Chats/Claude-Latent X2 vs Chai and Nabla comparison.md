## Bottom Line

**Chai-2 is not worse than open-source**—it's a genuine improvement over RFAntibody. 

**But Chai-2 may be the weakest of the three major closed platforms** (vs. Latent-X2 and Nabla) based on:
- Head-to-head target comparisons favoring Nabla
- Methodological criticisms about assay conditions
- No unique clinical de-risking data (like Latent-X2's immunogenicity)
- No unique modality (like Latent-X2's macrocycles or Nabla's GPCR agonists)

The $225M in funding reflects market positioning and investor enthusiasm for AI+bio, not necessarily technical leadership. For due diligence, I'd push hard on:
1. Why did Nabla find binders to targets Chai-2 failed on?
2. What are Chai-2's results under Nabla's assay conditions?
3. Where is Chai-2's immunogenicity/developability depth data?
