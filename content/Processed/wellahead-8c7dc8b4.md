# How It Works

1.  Wellahead receives lead information
2.  Wellahead sources options from our marketplace of vetted liquidity partners and surfaces the best deals available, tailored to each situation and stated preferences
3.  Customers select their preferred option and are introduced to a trusted liquidity partner
4.  Wellahead is paid a referral fee at introduction and/or at transaction close
