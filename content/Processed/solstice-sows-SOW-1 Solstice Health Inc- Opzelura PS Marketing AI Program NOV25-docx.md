Capitalized terms in this SOW shall have the meanings given to them in the Agreement unless defined herein.

## 1. Background

Company is engaged to provide access to its proprietary cloud-based AI-powered platform designed to assist customers with life sciences marketing asset generation and review (the “Platform”), along with optional managed services to accelerate the development of promotional material. The Platform includes functionality for customers to input their marketing assets, prompts, parameters, and other information (collectively, “Inputs") into the Platform to receive reports and feedback about such assets, modified versions of such assets, or new or additional assets (collectively, "Output").

## 2. Services

*   **AI-Powered Platform:** Cloud-based solution for generating and reviewing promotional and marketing assets tailored for pharmaceutical industry compliance
*   **Managed Services:** Dedicated team to assist in operating the platform, whose goal is to facilitate optimal results and compliance with industry standards, and create personalized configurations
*   **Authorized User Access:** Direct platform credentials for your designated team members

## 3. Deliverables

**Output:**

Description of Outputs are classified into projects that fall under three (3) categories:

*   Category 1 Project: A category 1 project deliverable describes an output from the Platform whereby the Platform updates an existing prior-approved asset. Examples include updating a prior-approved piece with new images, design components, copy, or label.
*   Category 2 Project: A category 2 project deliverable is a derivative digital marketing asset generated by the Platform that leverages prior-approved marketing materials or previously generated marketing assets. Examples include a rep-triggered email, a CRM email, a digital display asset, or a banner ad.
*   Category 3 Project: A category 3 project deliverable is a complex marketing asset generated by the Platform such as a deck, a commercial visual aid (CVA), a webpage, a leave behind, or any print piece.

## 4. Term

This SOW will commence on the SOW Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) year thereafter (the "SOW Term"). This SOW will remain in effect until the end of the SOW Term, unless earlier terminated as provided in the Agreement
