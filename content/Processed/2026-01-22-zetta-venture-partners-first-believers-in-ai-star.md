# Zetta Venture Partners | First Believers in AI Startups

Dylan Reid

Managing Director

[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/deploy/)

[Twitter](https://twitter.com/dreidco)

Dylan Reid is an investor in New York working with founders at the intersection of computing and the life sciences.

Before Zetta he worked at KEC Ventures where he launched their pre-seed program - investing in full-stack startups across biology, manufacturing, logistics and energy. He previously founded Matter.io, a venture-backed digital manufacturing company.

In addition to his work at Zetta, Dylan is involved in the NYC startup ecosystem as mentor to Techstars and CDL. He earned a bachelor’s degree from Cornell.
