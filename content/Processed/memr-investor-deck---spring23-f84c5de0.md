    *   EMR networks

2.  **Integration Layer:** APIs (receiving data from all sources)
3.  **Processing (Human + Digital):**
    *   Clinical Team
    *   Rx (presumably for prescription data)
4.  **AI/ML Processing:**
    *   OCR

    *   Data Storage

WE USE VARIOUS DIGITAL ON-RAMPS AND A HIGH-QUALITY OFFSHORE CLINICAL TEAM COUPLED WITH ML TOOLS TO RETRIEVE, SUMMARIZE AND STRUCTURE COMPREHENSIVE PATIENT RECORDS AT A FRACTION OF THE COST OF CURRENT SOLUTIONS
