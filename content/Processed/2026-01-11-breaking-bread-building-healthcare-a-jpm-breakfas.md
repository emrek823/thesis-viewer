About Event

​Join Inflect Health, Yosemite, and Reach Capital for a slower morning amid the JPM chaos.

​We're gathering a small group of founders and investors who are building healthcare's next chapter. Come connect over shared missions while enjoying coffee and pastries.

​**About the hosts**

​[Reach Capital](https://www.reachcapital.com/) is an early-stage VC team investing in founders who elevate human potential and ignite purpose across learning, health and work.

​[Inflect Health](https://www.inflect.health/) is an early-stage healthcare fund that brings to founders a network of 8,000+ clinicians across 50+ health systems.

​[Yosemite](https://yosemite.co/) is an oncology-focused venture fund investing in biotech and healthcare innovations to make cancer non-lethal.
