# Healthcare - Chief Medical Officer at Carbon Health
Interview conducted on June 30, 2025

## Topics
Technology Integration, Billing, Voice AI, Integration Process, Tech Solutions, Healthcare, Patient Engagement, Urgent Care

## Summary
The Tegus Client and Carbon Health expert discussed the use of AI technology, specifically Hello Patient, for outbound engagement in billing and revenue cycle management. The decision to choose Hello Patient was based on product experience, understanding of healthcare workflows, and ease of integration. While ROI is not yet quantified, the technology has the potential to improve efficiency and collect revenue that would have been previously written off. The expert praised the product's early experience, high execution velocity, and the founder's product intuition, expressing confidence in working with the Hello Patient team again and potentially investing in the company.

## Expert Details
Chief Medical Officer at Carbon Health. The expert works for a medical technology company that develops AI automation in-house. The expert is familiar and has evaluated many start ups in the space and are currently deploying a third party vendor.

Chief Medical Officer at Carbon Health. Reporting to the CEO, the expert is responsible for providing clinical and strategic leadership for the company, overseeing the intersection of care delivery, business operations, and technology development. The expert has financial and clinical performance responsibility for their care delivery business. Since Carbon Health has started licensing technology, the expert dedicates time to those products and business development efforts.

The expert can speak to Healthcare operations, Clinical Workforce Management and scaling, Clinical Program/Workflow/Protocol development, Health Tech product/software development, Revenue Cycle, Payer contracting, Health System Sales and Business development, and mergers and acquisitions of medical groups. Multi-state scaling and regulation. Experienced in both virtual and brick-and-mortar care delivery (including an integrated hybrid system). Current advisor for multiple digital health, and medical device companies.

Q: Please briefly describe your experience with AI tools used for inbound calls and customer service.
A: Since Carbon Health is a technology company, we develop internal AI tools for use. I have also evaluated many start ups in the space. We are currently deploying a third party vendor to help support with outbound activities for the group.

Q: Do you currently use any AI tools to automate inbound calls and/or customer service? Please list any you use or have evaluated.
A: To give you a sense of scale, we are using AI to support during 3000 encounters on a daily basis in 93 clinic sites across the country.

## Expert
Sure. I'm the chief medical officer at Carbon Health. I don't know if you're familiar with the company, but we're a health tech company that is a technology provider enablement platform. We have our own clinics that we support. We have 93 clinics that provide services in urgent care, primary care, behavioral health, some workplace health, as well as clinical research.
