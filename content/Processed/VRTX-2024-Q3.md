Lyrica is not approved for LSR in the US.

Q: What have you learned from the first commercial CASGEVY administration?A: Stuart Arbuckle, COO, noted the significant decision-making process for patients and physicians, the enthusiasm from authorized treatment centers, and the positive reception from payers and policymakers.
