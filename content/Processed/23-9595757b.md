## Slide 2: 2023: Company-wide objectives & key results that drive this narrative

**Build a profitable testing & care business**
1.  **Acquire customers sustainably**
    *   10% MoM new test customer growth
    *   $30 CAC
2.  **Increase 1-year CLV for each customer**
    *   Test AOV = Test COGS
    *   50% of customers (one off & members) order a second test within first 6 months
    *   15% increase in baseline care opt-in rate
    *   20% decrease in team time per customer

**Build other businesses on top of it**
3.  **Prove clinical utility**
    *   Establish cure & recurrence rates of Evvy's care model
    *   1 clinical referral or distribution partner (OBGYN / channel)
    *   1 partnership or study with fertility or pregnancy clinic (for improving outcomes)
    *   4 publications: 2 science, 2 clinical
    *   \*\*ensure partnerships are not all other digital health co's vs. established players
4.  **Acquire first customer of data platform**
    *   1st customer signed
    *   3 production-ready prediction models for high impact outcomes (responders / non responders)
    *   \*\*one of these 4 things needs to be beyond vaginitis

5.  **Continue building the foundations for a world-class team and company**
    *   20% increase in how we score each other across our values
    *   Data room and documentation processes shared company-wide
    *   HIPAA compliant

## Slide 13: Optimizing our test

*   **Expansion of proprietary reference database**
    *   Adding genomes to improve representation of microorganism diversity
    *   Supplement with findings from Evvy's metagenome pipeline
    *   Refining marker gene selection to improve specificity of microorganisms that are closely related
*   **Ongoing wet lab experiments**
    *   Mock community titration to establish limit of detection
    *   Metagenomics and qPCR comparison for detecting Candida species
*   **Antibiotic resistance**
    *   Adding a database for genes associated with antibiotic resistance in our samples
    *   This will enable us to identify antibiotic resistance from any bacteria in the sample to further personalize treatments offered through care

## Slide 16: Reminder: What is Clinical Care with Evvy?

Reminder: What is Clinical Care with Evvy?
Personalized, integrative, and supportive vaginal healthcare

*   Starts with The Evvy Vaginal Health test — at home testing that provides high fidelity test results and personalized, science-backed education on their vaginal health
    *   If results are eligible, you can decide to opt into clinical care through Evvy
*   Virtual, async visit with a clinician to design a personalized 2 month treatment program
*   Custom compounded prescription treatments sent to your door
*   Digital product to manage medications & calendar of what to do every day
*   Two 1-1 check-ins with certified health coach to provide support and make sure care fits into your life. Unlimited support via chat.
*   Community of other patients dealing with vaginal health issues
