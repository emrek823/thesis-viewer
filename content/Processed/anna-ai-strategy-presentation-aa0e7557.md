## Slide 3: Revenue Cycle Management
Revenue Cycle Management
ANNA's front-end RCM process can be completely automated, saving admin costs, improving time-to-service, and improving accuracy.

| | Benefit Check | Prior Authorization |
| :---------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **AI Functionality** | AI agent both scrapes web portal for benefits information and has a voice agent call in for eligibility and benefits, queuing up prior authorization submission | AI agent automates the completion of prior authorization, integrating into the EMR and submitting to the payor |
| **Benefits** | Seamless benefit check for a faster time-to-service and more accurate benefit check for prior authorizations, preventing denials | Seamless transition from intake to prior authorization with automated document retrieval improving time-to-service and conversion |
| **Status** | Live with Silna Health | Live with Silna Health |
