## Outside EMR Access Authorization and Conditions of Use Agreement

### Purpose:
The Purpose of this agreement is to Outline acceptable use conditions to ensure PHI is appropriately safeguarded, as well as providing a mechanism for outside Medical Staff and associated office staff to have authorized system access.

### Authorization Process:
Accurately and legibly complete the "Outside Medical Staff Authorization Form" attached in this agreement. Completed paperwork should be forwarded to the security officer at Maryland Oncology Hematology for processing. The Completed form can be mailed or faxed to

### Condition of Use:
* I understand that I will be accessing the EMR via a web-based browser and will ensure that I do not do so in a public venue to appropriately safeguard all the medical information, including Protected Health Information as that term is defined in the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act and its accompanying regulations ("HIPPA").
* I will Safeguard the privacy and confidentiality of all information accessed via the EMR and will not use or disclose said information except as permitted and in accordance with local, state and federal privacy and information system security mandates including, but not limited to HIPPA requirements and restriction.
* I will safeguard my system access credentials and not share such credentials with anyone else; thereby, not allowing anyone to impersonate me, borrow or otherwise utilize said credentials. I understand that I am responsible for any access to, alterations, or disclosures of PHI utilizing my access credentials.
* I will immediately report suspected security or privacy-related issues to Maryland Oncology Hematology Privacy Officer, including knowledge of any unauthorized access or use or knowledge of any other potential breach of PHI on or directly from the EMR.

## Outside EMR Access Authorization and Conditions of Use Agreement

* I will access only the PHI Associated with patients for whom I am directly and actively involved in their treatment or for whom I am authorized to provide medical consultation and will access and use such PHI only for these purposes.
