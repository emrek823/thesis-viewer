## Introducing Vivian Health Insurance

### I'm Expecting a Family

With a little one on the way you want to access care you know will help you like acupuncture or a doula.

**Vivian provides you with cash to spend on your health as you see fit.**

#### Legacy Plan

* Traditional health insurance plans will only pay for a limited amount of covered services.
* It’s your responsibility to sort through what is and is not covered and pay out of pocket for any non-traditional services.

#### Vivian

* You can use your funds to access care you know will help you.
* Our team will help you evaluate quality options and different costs, and use your available funds to pay for the care you want.

![Two older adults are practicing yoga on mats in a serene indoor home setting surrounded by lush plants. ### I'm Stressed & Overwhelmed

You are experiencing increased stress and anxiety and want to see a therapist you’ve met with before.

**Vivian won’t permission your care so you can go where you want to go.**

#### Legacy Plan

* Traditional health insurance plans often have limited coverage for mental health services, with long wait times for appointments and scarce access to in-network providers.

#### Vivian

* You can utilize your allocated funds to access mental health services promptly.
* Vivian's CareConcierge can assist in finding quality providers and you can simply pay for them with your available funds, no approval required.

![A smiling woman with glasses sits on a green couch at home, checking her blood sugar using a glucometer on her upper arm.](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20width='768'%20height='512'%20viewBox='0%200%20768%20512'%3E%3C/svg%3E "Diabetic,Woman,Using,A,Flash,Glucose,Monitor,To,Measure,Her")

### I Have Ongoing Health Needs

When you have a chronic condition there’s a lot to manage: regular check-ups, medication refills, and support in managing your condition.

**Vivian delivers personalized care that supports you at every stage.**

#### Legacy Plan

* Traditional plans may dictate where you receive care and require step therapy before approving certain medications or treatments, leading to delays and frustration.
* Worse, you are the one that has to figure out what steps to take to get the care you need.

#### Vivian

* Vivian's CareConcierge team helps you choose providers and treatments based on your preferences and needs.
* Your CareConcierge builds a relationship directly with you to understand goals. From there your CareConcierge will frame personal options to best meet your needs.

![A young girl in a hospital bed listens to her own heartbeat with a stethoscope while a healthcare professional, wearing scrubs and glasses, sits beside her. A medical monitor is visible in the background.](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20width='768'%20height='512'%20viewBox='0%200%20768%20512'%3E%3C/svg%3E "Female,Doctor,Working,With,Little,Girl,In,Hospital,Room")

### I Need Emergent Care

When things don’t go as planned, you find yourself in a sudden medical emergency, wanting immediate and trusted care.

**Vivian simplifies your healthcare experience.**

#### Legacy Plan

* Traditional insurance plans may involve high out-of-pocket costs for emergency services, with potential denials for coverage of certain treatments or procedures that take you months to process and appeal.

#### Vivian

* You have peace of mind knowing that your allocated funds can be used to cover emergency medical expenses.
* Your CareConcierge will help you evaluate follow-up options with your providers and help frame decisions around your emergent care.
