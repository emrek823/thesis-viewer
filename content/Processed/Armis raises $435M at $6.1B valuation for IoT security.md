# Armis Raises $435M at $6.1B Valuation

Armis raised $435M in funding at $6.1B valuation, with healthcare device security representing ~25% of revenue.

## Funding Details

- **Amount:** $435M
- **Valuation:** $6.1B
- **Lead Investor:** Goldman Sachs Alternatives Growth Equity
- **Participants:** CapitalG (Alphabet)
- **Prior Round:** $4.5B valuation (July 2025)

## Revenue & Growth

- **ARR:** $300M (July 2025), up from $200M (August 2024)
- **$100M ARR milestone:** Early 2023
- **Medical device security:** ~25% of revenue
- **IPO target:** 2026-2027

## Healthcare IoT Security Market

- Medical device security is fastest-growing segment
- Hospital networks average 10-15 connected devices per bed
- Legacy device vulnerability creates recurring demand
- Regulatory pressure (FDA cybersecurity requirements) accelerating adoption
