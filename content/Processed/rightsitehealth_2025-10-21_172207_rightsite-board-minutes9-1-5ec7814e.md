### Proceedings:
A meeting of the Board of Directors (the “Board”) was called to order at 1:00 p.m. CT by Jamo Rubin. There was a sufficient number of Board Members present to constitute a quorum. The present board members approved the minutes from the prior meeting.

Jamo began the meeting by reviewing preliminary August results and Q3 Company priority results.

Clayton reviewed key payor activities and EMS agency updates. The Board asked questions and engaged in discussion on these topics. Clayton mentioned that Paul McBride, an HC9 LP, has agreed to collaborate with RightSite.

Jamo reviewed Care Team operations, and the Board asked questions and engaged in discussion about these initiatives.

The meeting was adjourned at 2:00 p.m. CT.
