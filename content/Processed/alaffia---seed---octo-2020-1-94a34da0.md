## Slide 8: Go-to-Market Strategy

### Go-to-Market Strategy

**Public Insurance Agencies**
*   **Direct-to-Payer**
    Sell services to state-level Medicaid & federal-level Medicare
*   **Sub-Contract**
    Work alongside Recovery Audit Contractors (RACs) with existing multi-year auditing contracts

**Private Health Plans**
*   **Sell Up-Market**
    900+ Health Plans in the U.S. with outsourced improper payment recovery initiatives
*   **Competitive Pricing**
    Gain market share by offering less expensive & more customer-specific pricing options
