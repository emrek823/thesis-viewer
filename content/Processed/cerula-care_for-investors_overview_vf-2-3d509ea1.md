**THE PROBLEM**

*   **68M**: people have an advanced chronic condition managed by a specialist as their point of ongoing care
*   **72%**: vs. 31% across the whole population have a behavioral health comorbidity (roughly 49M)
*   **155M**: Americans do not have access to behavioral health professionals
