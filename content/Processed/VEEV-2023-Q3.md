## Summary

Veeva Systems reported strong Q3 results, exceeding guidance with a revenue of $552 million, up 16% YoY.  A key strategic update involves migrating Veeva CRM to the Veeva Vault platform, which will result in cost savings after 2025, and early adopters in 2024. Veeva continues to focus on innovation, strategic partnerships, and addressing the clinical data management space.
