### Company Overview

#### Vision & Positioning

- Named Andel after Scandinavian Cooperative Movement
- Anti-corporate ethos in US insurance world
- Thesis: GLP-1s are first wave of uninsurable drugs under current system
- Focus on population-level drug solutions vs individual treatments
- Target drugs cost ~$15K/year

#### Business Model

- Non-dispensing pharmacy model
- Buy direct from manufacturers as new distribution channel
- Target employers not covering specific drug classes
- Not competing with PBMs or selling directly to consumers
- Working with plan sponsors for particular drug classes
- Partnership with Omnicom GLP-1 practice provides channel to pharma companies

#### Market Strategy

- Similar to Spotify model for music industry consolidation
- Alternative to fragmented direct programs like NovoCare, LilyDirect
- B2B2C model focused on plan sponsor relationships
- Strong first-mover advantage with plan sponsors
- Three key pillars:
	- Pharma marketing
	- Consumer aggregation
	- Pharmacy rails
