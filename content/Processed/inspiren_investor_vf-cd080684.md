## Slide 15: Case Study Summary - The Valley Hospital

**Site:** The Valley Hospital
**No. of Beds:** 86
**Go-Live:** Oct 20, 2021
**Duration:** 16 months

**Patient Behavior**
*   **102** Avg. alerts per day (Low frequency/highly accurate)

**Staff Behavior**
*   **295** Live Views per Day
*   **1.2 min** Avg. Response Time

**Patient Safety**
*   **Increase in Bedside Shift Report & Rounding** (Statistically Significant)

**Tangible Results**
*   **45%** Reduction in Falls
*   **36%** Reduction in Falls with Injury

**AUGi's Added Value**

**Improved Nursing Practice**
*   33% decrease in staff response time to alerts
*   Sustained rounding practices even at the height of Omicron surge
*   Use of Live View in response to Call Bell activations

**Labor Savings**
*   Increased patient check-ins with remote Live View
*   Remote bed alarm checks from the central nursing station using Live View
*   Room visual context to alerts for remote response triage
*   Use of staff location in rooms for routing tasks to available nurses

**Quality & Risk Management**
*   Zero unwitnessed events with AUGI Event Review reporting
*   New training & incident response
*   Eliminating post-event unnecessary testing
