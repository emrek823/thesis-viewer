# Ambience Healthcare, Inc. - Clinical Director of Informatics &#x26; Analytics at John Muir Health

## Interview conducted on May 13, 2025

### Topics
- Clinical Documentation
- Medical Coding
- EHR Systems
- EHR Integration
- Service Codes
- Coding Team
- Risk Adjustment
- Market Research

### Summary
The Tegus Client engaged in conversations with an expert who is a family nurse practitioner and clinical director of informatics and analytics in a health system with multiple hospitals and outpatient clinics. The discussions focused on the benefits of Ambience in the coding process, including its accuracy rate, time-saving benefits, and suggested diagnoses. The expert also highlighted the need for Ambience to recommend procedure codes in addition to E&#x26;M codes, as well as the use of the Optum tool for risk adjustment and gap analysis. The conversation also touched on handling denials from payers, the hierarchy of different teams within the organization, and insights on AI trends and adoption in healthcare.

### Expert Details
**Clinical Director of Informatics &#x26; Analytics at John Muir Health**

The expert has been at the company since 2018. The expert is responsible for overseeing the implementation of electronic medical records systems, overseeing the execution of products, co-design of products, and EHR integration within the company. The expert is a customer of Ambience Health and was one of the key decision-makers to bring on their solution (along with the innovation team).

**Sean Doolan**
**Urgent Care Nurse Practitioner at John Muir Medical Group, a part of John Muir Health, since 2019.**
The expert also has a certificate in AI in healthcare.
