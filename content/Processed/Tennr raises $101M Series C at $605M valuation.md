# Tennr Raises $101M Series C at $605M Valuation

Tennr, a healthcare technology company automating referral management and administrative workflows using AI, closed a $101 million Series C funding round.

## Funding Details

- **Amount:** $101M Series C
- **Valuation:** $605M
- **Lead Investor:** IVP
- **Participants:** Andreessen Horowitz, Lightspeed, GV, ICONIQ, Foundation Capital, Frank Slootman
- **Total Raised:** $162M to date

## Company Performance

- Revenue tripled since $37M Series B (just two quarters prior)
- Processes 10 million documents per month
- Works with hundreds of healthcare organizations, thousands of providers

## Technology

Tennr uses proprietary orchestration engine and domain-specific language models (RaeLM) to:
- Process referrals faster
- Reduce denials
- Deliver transparent experience for patients and specialists

## Use of Funds

- Scale orchestration platform
- Expand Tennr Network
- Grow engineering and go-to-market teams
