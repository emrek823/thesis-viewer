*   Takes a **long time (90-120) days** to credential and onboard providers
*   Lack of accurate and real-time information available for agents at contact centers leads to **higher average handle times** and **lower first call resolution** of issues

*   Realtime credentialing system using RPA reduces time to **credential providers to less than 3 days**
*   Improve member, provider and group NPS by using our omni-channel AI contact center that **improves average handle times and first call resolution by 50%**
