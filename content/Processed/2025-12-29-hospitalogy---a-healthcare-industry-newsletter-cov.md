## Meet Blake Madden

Blake Madden is a former healthcare consultant and founder of Hospitalogy, a healthcare newsletter compiling the latest trends across M&A, strategy, finance, and markets into a simple-to-digest format.  
  
Blake’s expertise and insight come from his healthcare consulting, M&A, and transaction advisory background.   
  
Blake graduated from the University of Texas with a Bachelor’s in Business Administration and a focus on General Finance.
