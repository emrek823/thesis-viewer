# Adoption of Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare Delivery Systems: Early Applications and Impacts

> Source: [https://phti.org/ai-adoption-early-applications-impacts/](https://phti.org/ai-adoption-early-applications-impacts/)

![Featured Image](https://phti.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2025/03/Adoption-of-Artificial-Intelligence-in-Healthcare-Delivery-Systems-Early-Applications-and-Impacts.png)

Early adopters of “ambient scribe” technology—using artificial intelligence (AI) to take notes during patient appointments—report that these tools likely improve clinician burnout, but the financial impact is unclear, according to the report. The report is based on extensive interviews and convenings with health system leaders, leading AI companies, and industry experts participating in [PHTI’s AI Taskforce](https://phti.org/collaboration/ai-taskforce/).
