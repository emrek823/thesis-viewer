## Slide 7: The market is fragmented, frustrated and full of opportunity for innovation.
The market is fragmented, frustrated and full of opportunity for innovation.

Clinicians spend hours manually entering data for studies.
# data points per Ph III trial*: 3.5m
Mins effort per data point: 3
Clinical cost per hour: $60 x
Transcription cost per trial: $10.5m
*on an average oncology study

Data duplication costs 20% of a trial's budget & wastes >15% of a study's time.

Bringing new therapeutics to the market quicker has a huge revenue impact for pharma, delaying generic competition and potentially increasing market share.

Hospitals need a better way of running trials.
IgniteData

Pharma need to test drugs faster & cheaper.
