## Slide 6: Problem

*   Nearly **80%** of all trials **fail** to meet their enrollment deadline.¹
*   **9 out of 10** trials have to **double** their original timeline to meet their enrollment goal.²
*   **55%** of trials are **terminated** for failure to achieve enrollment.³
*   **37%** of sites under-enroll patients and **11%** fail to enroll even a **single** patient.²
*   Trial delays cost sponsors between **$600K** and **$8M per day** in potential revenue.⁴

## Slide 8: Solution – A Staged Approach

**Diagram:**
A central "Seer" entity is connected to four outer entities representing market segments in different stages.

**Stage 1: Sponsors & CROs**
*   Seer is the most trusted resource for where sponsors and CROs can and should do clinical research.

**Stage 2: Sites**
*   Seer is the most trusted resource for what clinical research sites can and should participate in.

**Stage 3: Patients**
*   Seer is the most trusted resource for what innovative therapies are available to patients and where to obtain them.

**Stage 4: HCPs (not participating in clinical research)**
*   Seer is the most trusted resource for what innovative therapies HCPs can and should offer to their patients (i.e. CRAACO).
