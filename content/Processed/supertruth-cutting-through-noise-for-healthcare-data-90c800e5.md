## Slide 5: Clarify: Non-clinical barriers to care (SDoH) adding value for biopharma

**Clarify: Non-clinical barriers to care (SDoH) adding value for biopharma**

**$300M revenue potential for hematology infusion therapy identified by large pharma**

**CHALLENGE**
*   Inability to quantify real-world treatment patterns with existing assets to assess performance and differentiate
*   Blind to non-clinical factors which impact care, creating a major risk at the time of launch

**IMPACT**
*   14K patients precisely identified, which enabled the marketing and patient support teams to prioritize investments
*   50% of those patients were likely lack access to treatment based on their access to transportation and proximity to CoEs
*   $300M in revenue potential by improving launch strategy and access

**SOLUTION**
ⓘ Clarify Journeys

*   **Map:** "SCD Patient Distribution by Zip" showing geographical distribution.

    *   A: "SCD Center in Zip" (Include, No SCD Center in Patient Zip)
    *   B: "SCD Severities" (Include, Severe)
    *   C: "Car Ownership" (Include, Does not own Car)
    *   + Filter
*   **Patient Segments Flow:** A flow chart showing segments of patients.

**Key Findings:**
*   Surfaced the first known national characterization of clinical and social profiles in the disease state
*   Precisely identified niche patient populations, including those with non-clinical barriers to care
*   Visualized patient journeys within high need precision cohorts across social and clinical factors
