### R1 CEO Joe Flanagan and Palantir CEO Dr. Alex Karp spoke to Bloomberg News March 14

In their interview, the pair discuss their exclusive partnership that will transform financial outcomes for healthcare providers. See what they had to say below and [watch the full interview](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/videos/2025-03-14/palantir-ceo-alex-karp-on-r1-palantir-partnership-video) on Bloomberg’s site.

Join us in embracing the future of healthcare, where advanced AI technologies lead to better products at lower costs, benefiting both patients and providers.

#### AI is an unstoppable trend, and our partnership with R1 is a shot over the bow. We’re transforming a complex business where users will get better outputs, and the economics of the business will be much better.”

##### Alex Karp, Palantir CEO

Bloomberg News Interview March 14

#### We can transform an institution from great to dominant. Our partnership with R1 is about changing the unit economics of the business by up to 50%. These are world-class players with ambition like no other.”

##### Alex Karp, Palantir CEO

Bloomberg News Interview March 14

#### The current system forces physicians to spend excessive time on administrative tasks, leaving patients confused when they need clarity the most. Our partnership aims to solve this problem by transforming the healthcare system with large language models and ontology.”

##### Joe Flanagan, R1 CEO

Bloomberg News Interview March 14

#### Today’s technology architecture isn’t designed to harness the opportunity to orchestrate, configure and inject intelligence into the enterprise. Palantir’s platform is very unique, and we’re excited to combine our talents with theirs. This partnership is about redefining the process for the industry with speed and shared ambition.”

##### Joe Flanagan, R1 CEO

Bloomberg News Interview March 14
