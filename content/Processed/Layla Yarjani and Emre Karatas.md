### Company Background & Funding Round

- Layla Yarjani (Founder) - 5.5 years at Accenture, second-time founder (first company in education)
- Personal motivation: Overcame nocturnal panic attacks through integrative medicine after conventional providers only offered SSRIs
- Co-founder Rocky - former #2 physician at Andrew Weil Center (10+ years), led research on integrative primary care outcomes
- Current round: $2M raise led by Tracy Chadwell from 1843 Capital (also in Ripple, Recuro, Midi Health, Function Health) at $6.5M pre-money
- Previous angel funding raised for initial product development

### Product & Customer Traction

- Business-in-a-box MSO for physicians/NPs wanting to build integrative/functional medicine practices
- Educational go-to-market strategy: 10-week business bootcamp
	- 65 applications, 44 paid customers (all Andrew Weil Center alumni except 1 psychiatrist)
	- 23 customers on MSO waitlist, 1 clinic already signed under consulting agreement
- Customer spectrum ranges from 7-figure Texas practice adding membership component to new NP practices starting from scratch
- Key customer pain points addressed:
	- Collaborative care team design and referral workflows without fax/BAA headaches
	- Care plan builder (currently done via email with supplement links)
	- Outcomes measurement and patient tracking through integrated app
- Technology integrations: EHR partnerships (starting with Elation), Aura/Apple Health, Zeus platform
- AI component prioritizes specific journals/articles flagged by Rocky for integrative care plans

### Market Opportunity & Growth Strategy

- Target market: 250,000 PCPs in private practice
	- 50% already recommend complementary medicine
	- 40,000+ have completed integrative/functional medicine training
- Phase 1 (current $2M round): 200 clinics at $25K ACV through existing trained provider network
- Phase 2 expansion strategies:
	- Target residency programs and NP programs for younger providers
	- “Matchmaker” model connecting retiring boomer practice owners with younger providers
	- Practice acquisition/transition facilitation with MSO implementation
- Revenue model includes payer reimbursement, membership programs, and HSA-advantaged offerings
