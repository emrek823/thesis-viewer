### Product & Market

- Problem: Payers have ~1,000 policies per product spread across websites
- Market Dynamic: ~200 policy changes occur nightly
- Current Product: Semi-structured data including:
	- Raw policy text
	- Key metadata (HCPCS codes, ICD-10 codes, effective dates)
- Product Roadmap: Moving toward fully structured data models that represent:
	- Complete payer logic for specific services
	- Example: Humira coverage requires integration of:
		- Prior authorization policy
		- Step therapy policy
		- Quantity limits
		- Site of care requirements
