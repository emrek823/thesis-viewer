# CLARA

## Mission Statement
Help Americans age in peace and dignity by reimagining home care

## Product Overview
Clara is an in-home senior care discovery, matching and management platform for consumers. It's the "operating system" for home care

We combine a curated supply of independent care workers with AI-powered matching models and consumer-friendly workforce management software for a 360° home care solution at 20-40% lower cost than traditional agencies

## Team
Jon Levinson is a seasoned leader with 10+ years of product experience, from pre-seed startups to large-cap public companies. He most recently served as Uber Direct's first product lead, driving hyper-growth from $11M -> $1B ARR

Ian Gillis is a former software engineering leader with experience at Facebook, Addepar, and Uber. He most recently led a team of 220 at Restaurant Brands International.

## Target Market

### Immediately Addressable Market
*   2-3M private-pay, American consumers seeking in-home non-medical care for loved ones
*   $20-$30B/year, growing >7% CAGR for next 10+ years

### Target Customer
*   55-75 y.o. affluent, predominantly white, female
*   Describe agencies as "overpriced and unreliable"
*   Uncomfortable paying under the table, unsure of how to legally manage employment logistics

## Revenue & Expenses

### Revenue
*   Supply-side listing fee (~$30/month/care worker)
*   10% take rate on earnings (~$400/month/consumer)
Will hit $1M ARR at <200 consumers & 20 paying care workers

### Expenses
*   Marketing & R&D
*   SaaS: Will leverage for core features to accelerate GTM, (e.g. embedded payroll) (~$25/month/consumer)
*   Care worker background checks (~$100/check)

## Marketing & GTM Strategy
Adjusted "come for the tool, stay for the network" approach:
*   V1 launch w/ management tool: consumer acquisition via digital advertising, followed by aggressive lean into channel partnerships w/ hospital networks
*   Seed supply side w/ workforce management offering (consumers onboard workers found off-platform, providing high-quality, warm leads for marketplace)
*   Supplement supply via digital advertising, job listings, and partnerships in care worker communities
*   Fast-follow w/ marketplace offering
