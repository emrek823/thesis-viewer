## Slide 13: Architecture

\*In conversation with Yale

# Architecture

**(Diagram)**

*   **Input:**
    *   Prescription (from Doctor)
    *   Payor Clinical Policies
*   **Core System:** LATENT
*   **Data Flow:**
    *   Prescription -> Epic
    *   Epic -> PA Specialist
    *   Epic also feeds into LATENT via:
        *   MedicationRequest (CDS Hook)
        *   Patient Notes (FHIR v4)
        *   SMART on FHIR (HyperSpace)
    *   Payor Clinical Policies -> LATENT
*   **LATENT Capabilities:**
    *   AI Bias Monitoring
    *   HIPAA Compliant
*   **Output:**
    *   LATENT -> Any Output (pdf, ePA, Epic)
