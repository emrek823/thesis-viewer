## Slide 3: Data Flows

**echo**

**Top Flow (Existing Process):**

1.  **Child is diagnosed.**
2.  **Provider Submits Pre-authorization Request** (existing process)
3.  **Optum Pre-Auth Received.**
4.  **Optum Informs Echo Of A New Autism Patient**

**Bottom Flow (Echo Process):**

*   **Optum**
    *   Caregiver enrolled in Echo and completes Caregiver Assessment
    *   **Allows for targeted treatment plans**
    *   *(Data flow arrow)*
*   **Provider**
    *   Provider completes client assessment and transmits on Echo Provider Portal
    *   *(Data flow arrow)*
*   **Echo Clinical Screening**
    *   Echo's proprietary AI insights + team of specialists review each assessment for progress / regression
    *   *(Data flow arrow)*
*   **Optum**
    *   Optum receives assessment including Echo's clinical team review on Echo payer portal
    *   *(Data flow arrow to Optum Optional Integration)*
*   **Optum (Optional Integration)**
    *   Optionally, Echo can integrate directly with Optum systems to transmit assessment

**Data Transfer Methods Indicated:**
*   SFTP
*   API

**Overall Process Note:**
Echo's clinical specialists makes recommendations to health plans to assist in claim decision.
