October 2025

Summary

October was a big month for Gyde. We signed our first two LOIs (Avid, Benavest), and received a verbal confirmation to move to a third (Capstone). We shipped our first AI application and are now live with our design partners. Finally, we signed a preemptive term sheet with Lightspeed for our Series A and are now actively working to add another anchor capital partner to the round. We welcome questions, but would ask that this not be socialized externally. While we want to remain largely silent in terms of major public announcements, we launched our homepage to strong reviews from external stakeholders. As we move towards 2026, our focus will now shift to filling key leadership roles (e.g. CTO) and executing our Value Creation plan for our acquired partners.

The combination of Avid, Benavest, and Capstone will bring us an estimated $55-$60M in revenue and ~$5M in EBITDA as we enter 2026. While we acknowledge our mid-funnel performance is strong, we still believe we are underperforming in driving top-of-the-funnel action. Our model relies on our ability to execute a high-velocity M&A motion, which requires significant top-of-the-funnel lead generation.
