## Slide 3: Program for All Inclusive Care for the Elderly (PACE)
Program for All Inclusive Care for the
Elderly (PACE)

*   Developed over 40 years ago in San Francisco and just recently opened to for-profits to expand access
*   Cares for elderly (Medicare eligible), socioeconomically vulnerable (Medicaid eligible), and frail (nursing home eligible)
*   PACE is a health insurance Plan and long-term care insurance Plan – fully at risk for the total cost of care
*   PACE also provides all the medical and social services needed to stay out of the hospital and in your home

### Making an Impact (Case Study)
Prior to enrolling in PACE, participant had frequent and lengthy hospitalizations, mostly due to open wounds and subsequent infections. He was wheelchair dependent and had severe anxiety about leaving home.

Since enrolling in PACE, the care team has provided state-of-the-art wound care on his legs, and pro-actively treated his infections. With physical therapy and encouragement, he is now using a four-wheel walker, one of his personal independence goals. We have connected him to food banks and long-term disability coverage, and he has expressed tremendous gratitude toward his caregivers.
